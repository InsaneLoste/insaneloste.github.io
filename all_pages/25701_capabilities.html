The user of this ability is a supreme force that should not be underestimated. As with any dhampir a user can not simply achieve this level of power. It all depends on who the dhampir has inherited their vampire side from. Naturally with a transcendent parent, the dhampir will also be transcendent. As with most transcendent beings a transcendent dhampir is able to alter reality on a universal scale. Also the normal dhampir abilities are enhanced to absolute levels.
