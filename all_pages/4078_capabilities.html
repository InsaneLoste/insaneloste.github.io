The user possesses powers that are tied and otherwise bound to their personal chastity; as users are in a state of being "pure", so to are their powers that are tied to it. If the user should ever engage in an intimate relationship, their powers could weaken or be completely lost altogether. However, if they should continue to abstain from any intimate activities, their powers will remain intact and even place them at the pinnacle of their potential, which could even lead towards a divine status of their own.
