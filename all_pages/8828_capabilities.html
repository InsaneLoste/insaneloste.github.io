A user with this ability either is or can transform into an envy deity, a deity associated with the envy, jealousy, resentment, bitterness, discontent, grudges, and spite and all their aspects. This grants them absolute knowledge and power over them, their concepts and principles. They are often worshiped by those be it individually or as a group, who possessed considerable jealousy and envy. Others who are less envious might worship this deity to merely placate them. Envy deities weren't a common feature of worship in polytheistic religions but were important among ancient and current civilizations.
