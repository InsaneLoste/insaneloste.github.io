• While much more clever and capable of facing off against dangerous opponents many giant slayers are still mortal, thus must rely on a combination of wit and skill to survive against near-impossible odds.
  • Transcendent giants are beyond most giant slayers capability (unless they deploy divine aid or come across transcendent defying technologies / weaponry).
  • May need some skills/powers to kill some types of giants.
    • Divine Slayer to giant gods.
    • Demonic Slayer to demonic giants.
    • Mage Slayer to giant mages.
      • Transcendent giant mages may difficult to slay.
    • Undead Slayer to undead giants.
    • Monster Slayer to giant monsters.
