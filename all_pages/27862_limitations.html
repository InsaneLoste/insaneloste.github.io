• Supernatural Hunter are capable of defeating Zompires.
  • Acidic Blood, Poisonous Blood, and perhaps Vampiric Toxicity can be counteractive abilities against them.
  • Like Vampires, they may need some form of life-forces, otherwise they may cease to function completely, age rapidly, or suffer starvation.
  • Destroying the brain, decapitation, and a wooden stake to the heart may kill a Zompire.
  • Conversion Negation can cure Zompires.
  • May still be idiotic and/or mindless.
