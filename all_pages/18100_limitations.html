• Peak-human capabilities are only near-enhanced, meaning users are either almost or at least partially able to perform feats beyond human limits, but usually not consistently or without immense strain and effort depending on the exact extent of their individual capabilities.
  • Users of Enhanced, Supernatural and Absolute Condition can easily overpower, outperform, and outsmart peak humans.
  • Some users may need a special Training Regimen to maintain their condition (or at least for their physical aspects).
  • Like all humans, the user will usually slow down and become less effective with age (starting in mid-30s or so, in most verses) unless they are an Old Prime or have some other superhuman ability to counter the aging process.
