The user can utilize the Fairy Arts, a specialized system of techniques based around the powers of fairies. This set of skills is focused on channeling fairy essence, forces, magic, energy, elements, spirits, items, and/or possibly calling on fairy to perform various feats. Practitioners may or may not be fairy or fairy in origin. Whatever the circumstance, utilization of these of these arts enables one to exhibit unique capabilities such as conjuring fairy elements, supernaturally enhancing one's body/abilities and even accessing fairy materials.
