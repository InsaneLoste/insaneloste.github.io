User has their entire physiology completely enhanced with magic; being empowered by magical energies, forces, items and possible other arcane beings. While the source of the enhancement was magical, the powers bestowed may be completely non-magic in nature. This trait often results in greater strength, stamina, agility, intelligence, and other powers associated with the mind and body. Through the magic they are imbued with, some users may use their embed magical energy to enhance themselves and achieve new forms of magical abilities that enhanced and redefines their bodies through sheer magic alone, re-sculpturing their physical status and power level depending on how much and what kind magic they have.
