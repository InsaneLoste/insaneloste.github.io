The character is a fortune deity, a god/goddess who represents or is associated with fortunes, luck, chance, success, misfortunes, and even destiny and wealth. Fortune Deities are a combined version of fate and wealth deities as they are able to affect both as it pertains to affecting fortunes. However, Fortune Deities are a slightly less powerful than Fate Deities as they can't affect fate and destiny on such a grand scale. Fortune Deities are common in mythology and were important among ancient and current civilizations.
