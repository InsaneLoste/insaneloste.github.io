The user can create, shape and manipulate hellish poison, capable of necrotizing, paralyzing and/or intoxicating anyone or anything in is path. In addition, demonic poison is capable of inflicting excruciating pain, both physically and spiritually tormenting the victim. It has the potential to corrupt one's soul, and is very effective against the holiest of supernaturals, such as angels. More powerful users can even ultimately summon/resurrect/create evil spirits, elemental entities and/or demons from Hell.
