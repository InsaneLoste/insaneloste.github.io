Users wield enigmatic forces of extreme power, capable of seeking out and bond with agent(s) as their hosts. These enigma forces possess a mysterious and puzzling nature, making them challenging to comprehend for many. With the ability to manipulate and affect anything in their existence, they bend to the will of their hosts, fulfilling their whims and desires. Depending on their origin, they can be sentient powers and living energies, handpicking their hosts, or special sources of energy shrouded in unknown origins, controlled solely by their chosen hosts.
