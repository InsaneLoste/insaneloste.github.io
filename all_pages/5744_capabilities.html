User can utilize a malefic form of Alchemy, the Mystic and Scientific pursuit of the power of the Philosopher's Stone and Universal Panacea, focusing on Elemental Transmutation, ranging from transforming base materials into purer e.g. lead or iron into silver or gold, to making permanent changes on the state of matter e.g. making glass malleable or durable, various forms of Elemental Manipulation, Life-Force Manipulation such as Immortality, Healing and even Life Creation or at the least Clones/Homunculi. They also know how to create Potions for rejuvenation and immortality and Magical Items.
