User has an intelligence/mind that operates on a level that exists beyond normal eldritch minds, making them exist in a state beyond the conception of intelligence, logic, reason or thought. A comparison between the two would be like the difference between a puddle of water and the ocean. Examples of such fathomless minds can be found most commonly in beings possessing a Cosmic Otherness, as well as occasionally in extreme instances of eldritch hive minds and other such entities.
