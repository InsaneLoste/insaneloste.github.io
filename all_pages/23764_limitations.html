• Immutability/Transmutation Immunity prevents transformation.
  • May require use to know the "science" behind the target they are changing.
  • Transmutation that associated with another ability like Reality Warping can be highly dangerous as it gives them more range to affect objects, including themselves.
  • Transmutation Reversal, Restoration, Flawless Restoration or Absolute Restoration can undo the transmutation.
  • May be able to change only certain target types.
  • May be limited on how much matter can be changed at once.
