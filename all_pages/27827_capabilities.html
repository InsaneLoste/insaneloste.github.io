The user can utilize the Zodiac Arts, a specialized system of techniques based around zodiac power. Constellations and the horoscope systems associated with them have long been believed to hold influence over individuals’ lives, affecting everything from personalities to relationships to probabilities. In this instance, one can use training to tap into the essences of their respective zodiac to gain preternatural abilities. This can include transforming into entities associated with a particular sign, becoming enhanced by celestial forces or even channeling elemental power associated with said horoscope. While one is usually limited to one particular zodiac identity, potentially, one’s practice could lead them to gain the powers of other signs and reach greater levels of power.
