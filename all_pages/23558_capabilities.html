Users attain the epitome of balance with themselves, perfectly synchronized and harmonized with every aspect of their being. Their mind, spirit, body, and all other facets converge at a central point, allowing them to maintain tranquility even in the most dire situations. They possess the remarkable ability to channel the power of their unstable qualities, gaining complete control over themselves and achieving immunity to any form of imbalance. All inner conflicts are resolved, granting them balance with their higher, true self, and unlocking a vast array of Supernatural Powers.
