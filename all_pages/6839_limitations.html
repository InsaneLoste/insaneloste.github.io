• The user must often align themselves with the divine force they seek to summon, attempting to summon and use such beings for evil deeds can backfire enormously without sufficient effort by the summoner.
  • Many divine powers (especially those of monotheistic source) consider the use of direct name summoning a blasphemy unless granted permission by the deity or force in question, leading to potentially apocalyptic results (in this respect many divine names are also in some ways cursed names if used without proper care).
  • While one may summon a divine power or phenomena there is no guarantee one can control it.
