The user can create or wield weaponry with power over scalar waves, which grants the user a wide variety of MCE-based abilities, including use of many electromagnetic and advanced EM Wave emissions, from plasma to ionic and even spectra based effects. This ability is not only specific to mystic weapons, but also technologically advanced weapons. The power of the weapons depends on what they were created for or what level of scalar-based ability they possess, including over what forms of ionization or ions.
