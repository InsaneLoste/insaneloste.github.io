Users of this ability can either mimic the traits of a Taki Reio or are one, a Taki Reio is a powerful entity found in Japanese folklore that takes on the appearance of fearsome divine apparitions at the basins of holy waterfalls, they mimic the form of other deities and are considered the apex of the supernatural world, with all other demons, spirits and yokai bowing before their might as embodiments of the gods themselves.
