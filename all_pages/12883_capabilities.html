The users can form a personal and powerful union between them and an inner beast, and they both tend to protect and care for one another and assist each other in multiple situations. Users and beasts can also share an empowering friendship so that they can draw ferocious strength from and granting them the raw power to achieve anything and overcome potential obstacles in their way. The bond they have with their inner beast is extremely personal because the user and their inner beast are a part of one another and are able to synchronize each other and gain a variety of powers that they can use for themselves.
