The user can create drones; automated constructs serving as unmanned machines remotely controlled to act on their creator's behalf. They can intercept physical attacks, projectiles and other forms of harm. Depending on the user, the drones can be of any appearance, be they energy spheres or robotic fauna. They also often act on the offensive, attacking anything or anyone they designate as a threat to their creator. Said faculties also have simple day to day application usage as well, like housekeeping, homeland security or even good old fashioned detective work.
