• Anarchy is a vague term that can be interpreted in many different ways.
  • Users might be compelled to bring anarchy whether they wish to or not, even if it acts against their own interest.
  • Users may bring anarchy in spite of whatever consequences that follow, bringing more harm than good.
  • Users may be fundamentally unable to work for or with others as that is itself a form of control.
  • Users may be unable to give orders or commands.
