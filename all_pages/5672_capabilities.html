A user with this ability either is or can transform into a Cŵn Annwn, a white hellhound with red ears in Welsh mythology, and were associated with the wild hunt, controlled by their master, said to be either Arawn, king of Annwn in the First Branch of the Mabinogi and said to be in the Fourth, or by Gwyn ap Nudd as the underworld king and king of the fairy folk in medieval lore when a souls time has come due if that soul was a bad individual or had made a deal with the gods and crossed them then the hounds would come. The hunting grounds of the Cŵn Annwn are said to include the mountain of Cadair Idris, where it is believed the howling of these huge dogs foretold death to any living being who heard them. Their growling is loudest when they are at a distance, and as they draw nearer, it grows softer and softer. The coming of these bests is generally seen as a death omen.
