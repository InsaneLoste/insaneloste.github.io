Users of this power can combine infections, cure-alls, afflictions, remedies and malignancies of all kinds into all new contagions that are infinitely deadlier than their predecessors. The key difference between this and Power Mixture is that this ability focuses on the combination or recombination of certain pathogenic elements both natural and artificially created, like spores, molds, drugs, bacteria, inoculations and other beneficial/detrimental substances.
