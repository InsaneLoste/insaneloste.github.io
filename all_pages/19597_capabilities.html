The user possesses a power bond; a profound connection that links them to their abilities, whether these powers originate from an object, an innate talent, or even a supernatural source. This bond goes beyond mere control, fundamentally altering how they interact with and utilize their abilities. It signifies an inseparable and deep relationship between the user and their supernatural capabilities, regardless of their nature or form, whether it's a sentient power or an entity of power, and so on.
