Users, either innately or through training, is a master of wielding many types of clubs, among the simplest of all weapons: a short staff or stick, usually made of wood, wielded as a weapon, such as a mace, baton, bludgeon, truncheon, Cosh or nightstick inflict heavy blunt force damage and/or trauma, making the user very dangerous in close quarters combat.
