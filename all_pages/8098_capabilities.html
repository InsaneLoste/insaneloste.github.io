The user can utilize the Emotional Arts, a specialized system of techniques based around emotional abilities and empathic force. Emotions have long been believed to carry great power. They can be found in a variety of beings (animals, plants, spirits, demons, angels, gods, etc.). It is one of the few aspects that are found throughout the various realms. To that end, learning to take control of one’s emotions allows one to master one’s state of mind, instincts and reactions. One can also engage in techniques that can unlock special powers such as mood manipulation, emotional energy control, behavior alteration, etc.
