Users can control various futuristic lifeforms, creatures and species that have usually evolved from modern day creatures due to future events and changes to the environment. They control them to do their bidding like, help them during situations, use them against foes, use them to see locations and get information about a particular place, use them for battle, use them for spying on others. In some cases, they can even evolve creatures into future counterparts, as well as empathize and communicate with them.
