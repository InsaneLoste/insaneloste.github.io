• Users with Power Anchoring cannot discard any of their powers.
  • This power is dangerous as the user is unable to get their powers back unless they come across someone of Power Restoration.
  • Users cannot discard Omnipotent Powers.
  • The power may be uncontrollable, meaning it may function automatically, even if the user doesn’t want to discard their own abilities.
  • Physical powers like Auxiliary Organs and Bone Spike Protrusion for example, might be painful to discard.
