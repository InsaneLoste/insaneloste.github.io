User with this ability either is or can transform into an Akaname (or Filth Licker), a type of demonic spirit found in Japanese folklore and often appearing as an ugly, small humanoid with a horrendously enlongated tongue which it would use to feed on filth in neglected bathrooms. While relatively harmless to humans they were nevertheless frightening beings and harbingers of disease and misfortune, thus they were unwanted "visitors" to a household and a warning about keeping good hygiene.
