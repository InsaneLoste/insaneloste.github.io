Users of this ability can either mimic the traits of a Todaiki or are one, a Todaiki is a horrific cursed spirit from Japanese folklore that was once human but has been transformed via dark magic into a twisted humanoid version of a candlestick, often rendered immobile and mute with no means to escape their torture and being spirited away to far-away lands where they are used as macabre ornaments by sadistic sorcerers or evil spirits.
