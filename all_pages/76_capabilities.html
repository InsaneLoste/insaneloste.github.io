The user can utilize the Absolute Arts, a specialized system of techniques based around supreme, possibly omnipotent power. While there are instances of individuals who can acquire exponentially enhanced power regarding physical ability, matter and/or energy manipulation or mental/spiritual prowess, there are those whose abilities and/or potential can only be described as infinite. Some engage in training that allows them to tap into any and/or all preternatural forces, granting them unfathomable might. There are acolytes who use unique regimens that can grant them supernaturally unbound qualities (mind, speed, strength). There are also those who are inherently almighty regarding a particular facet of existence. These individuals operate at levels above and beyond what most can achieve or even imagine. They wield skills that are practically unstoppable, while often also possessing limitless possibilities for even greater power.
