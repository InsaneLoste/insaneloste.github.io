The user can create, shape and manipulate Femtomites, machines or robots whose components are at or near the scale of a femtometre. Theoretical devices ranging in size from the scale of trillionths of a meter or femtoscale 10^(-15) to 10^(-17) m. Usually fabricated from picoscale or atomic level outward. Such an effect enables users to manipulate the meta matter state on a particle to subquantum scale.
