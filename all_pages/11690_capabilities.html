The user can create, shape and manipulate heat by increasing the kinetic energy of atoms and thus making things hotter, ranging from subjective feeling of heat to absolute hot. It is the flow of energy between atoms themselves. This enables the user to control kinetic energy on an atomic level. From a culinary standpoint, this power excels in providing food among other luxuries. Warm temperature can be also be comforting, especially within colder climates and counteract cold-based illnesses. However too much heat can make a normal environment become stagnate, sweaty and generally unbearable. It can also induce fainting, Heat Stroke, and unhygienic odors to rise due to Dehydration.
