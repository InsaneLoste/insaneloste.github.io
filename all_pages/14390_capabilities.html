Users can control all kinds of lizards. Holders of this ability can order lizards to do various things, for example, summon them into battle to fight their enemies, or use them to infiltrate places that would normally be inaccessible to the user. Naturally, they could also communicate with them, read their thoughts and emotions and establish telepathic connections with them. More advanced users could create lizards out of thin air or even manipulate their genetic makeup and DNA to improve their characteristics and qualities.
