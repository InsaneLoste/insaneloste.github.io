Users of this ability are either a member of a demonic fairy group or can mimic the traits of them, in world folklore fairies come in three distinct kinds - the spirits of the dead, nature spirits and the more demonic spirits: the concept of truly demonic fairies grew as ancient pagan beliefs were changed over time to be more in line with monotheistic faiths but even in pre-Christian societies there existed a wide variety of monstrous fairies that would be considered "demonic" in the sense of being malignant entities that often sought undue harm upon mortals.
