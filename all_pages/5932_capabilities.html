The user can utilize the Death-Force Arts, a specialized system of techniques based around necrotic power. Death-Force is a defining feature of mortality; it acts as the energetic catalyst that governs phenomena such as decay, degradation and ultimately, death. It also connects one to the realms of the dead and their inhabitants. While magic can be a source of control for this power, it is not a necessity. What matters is that, through specialized training, one gains a unique insight into death-force to learn and manifest supernatural abilities and skills.
