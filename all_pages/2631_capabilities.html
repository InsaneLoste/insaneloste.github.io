The user can bio-energetically absorb/replicate any matter and energy. They can draw any material or force into themselves, making it their own. The user can then, replicate as well as manifest the properties of whatever they have assimilated into their system. They can also manipulate, augment, and alter what they’ve absorbed. The properties of the assimilated energy and matter are also encoded into the user. This allows them to manifest the aspects of anything they’ve previously consumed instead of having to constantly absorb it. At higher levels, users can absorb, replicate, augment, and manipulate conceptual things such as probability, space-time and reality.
