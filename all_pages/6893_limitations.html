• Djinni may be bound to a receptacle (usually imagined as a lamp), which they cannot leave without certain events happening (someone rubbing the lamp, certain commands, etc.).
    • Anyone who holds the receptacle may have command over the Djinni, forcing it to grant them wishes.
    • Destruction of the receptacle may either kill the genie or release it.
  • Shapeshifting must be related to Air.
