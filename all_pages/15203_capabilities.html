Weaker users, who can only change the states of matter, but cannot control it, have very basic uses, for example, the user could liquefy the ground to move faster over it, or by making enemies slip and fall or slowing them down, change the air from gaseous state to solid, and using it as a shield to protect themselves, converting gases to liquid to create for example water, freezing liquids to create solids, freezing air to create ice, transform the air into a gas or heating the air, even being able to create fire.
