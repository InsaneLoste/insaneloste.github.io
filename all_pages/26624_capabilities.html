The user with this ability either is or can transform into a Vanara, a member of a race of semi-divine forest/mountain dwelling anthropomorphic apes who played a very important role in the Ramayana when they helped Rama (7th avatar of Vishnu) fight the deadly battle against the rakshasa king Ravana in Lanka and liberate Sita, Rama's wife. They possess the typical features of monkeys/apes with human and godly attributes. They coexist with the Rikshas, a race of semi-divine bears.
