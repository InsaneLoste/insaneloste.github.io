• Fairy-vampires may be killed by wooden staking. Like ordinary fairies, this method makes a hybrid highly affected to Form/Absolute Restoration.
  • Despite being part fairy, hybrids are still vulnerable to Fire Attacks such as Supernova Inducement, but considering the speed in which they may recover from the burns, this power is not as lethal as it is for normal vampires.
  • Users can be restrained by objects enchanted by Magic, which can leave them susceptible to attacks from even younger vampires.
  • Due to their vampiric heritage, users may still be vulnerable to garlic.
