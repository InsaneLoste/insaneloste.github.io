• The user can resurrect endlessly however their resurrection can be manipulated just like normal resurrection
  • The user may still be able to be injured normally, and may feel the full pain of dying each and every time.
  • Users of Absolute Character Erasure/Absolute Erasure can permanently erase the user and prevent them from ever resurrecting.
  • Users of Unavertable Death and Resurrection Negation can make the death permanent.
  • Users of Infinity Bypassing can prevent resurrection.
  • Depending on the cosmology, this may be seen as a cursed state that eternally separates an individual from a universal salvation / truth.
  • May cause some individuals to go insane due to the trauma of continually returning to life, especially when the world begins to change dramatically over time (loved ones dying, entire civilizations coming and going etc).
