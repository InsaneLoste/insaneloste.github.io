Users can manipulate their own origin, their birth, events, form, abilities and powers, etc. that they choose as their own, regardless of lesser influences. Basically, this power allows one to write and rewrite everything that lead to them being who they are in the present moment, granting a near-indisputable sovereignty over every aspect of their existence. By denying something becoming a part of their origin, the user can prevent it from affecting them entirely, effectively denying causality, and by changing what is already a part of their origin, the user can change virtually anything about themselves.
