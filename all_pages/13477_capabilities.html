Users of this ability can either take on the traits of, or are a Katsura Otoko, a dangerous but seductive spirit in Japanese folk tales that takes the form of supernaturally beautiful men who project their image upon the moon, whenever a mortal gazes upon this image they grow hypnotized by their beauty - this causes the Katsura Otoko to begin to feed on their life-force, if a person stares too long they will inevitably drop dead.
