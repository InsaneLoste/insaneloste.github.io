The user is able to use a form of magic that allows them to cast magical spells in a close combat manner, either by directly attacking their opponents with magic or by integrating their magic with an existing martial art form. This way of utilizing magic enhances their combat skills significantly, whether they engage in unarmed combat or wield melee weapons. Because of their magical properties, not only can they do more damage and attack with more precision than normal, they can also harm magical beings that are typically immune to conventional forms of attack.
