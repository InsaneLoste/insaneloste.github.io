• Weapon Immunity
  • May be unable to create weapons, being limited to manipulating only from already existing sources.
  • Distance, mass, precision, etc. depend upon of the knowledge, skill, and strength of the user, and their power's natural limits.
  • May be limited to only manipulating certain types of weapons.
  • May be limited to only weapons that have been previously held, seen, or scanned (i.e., weapons in the user's memory).
