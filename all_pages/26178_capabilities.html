The user can cause absolute death to absolutely anyone or anything without any exceptions, including immortal, "Nigh-Unkillable" beings, Death itself & even otherwise "unbirth" beings, although the exact method & ease varies by the fine details of the power variant. Since the death would be unavoidable and permanent, killing literally anything in existence they desire, whether it be a person, an object, a god, the user is not impeded by any defense or obstacle when it comes to killing their enemy, the sole exceptions being Omnipotent Beings and users of Absolute Immortality. Anything that was killed through the use of this power can not be resurrected.
