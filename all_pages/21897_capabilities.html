The user can gain powers by symbiotically hosting or linking to a science-based/scientific force being. The entity can come in a variety of forms. It can be a scientifically-altered or empowered being. It can be an existence that has specialized control and/or dominion over certain fields (biology, chemistry, physics, etc.). It could even be an embodiment of science itself. In connecting to this entity, the host is capable of exhibiting potent abilities related to scientific disciplines, depending on its overall nature. This can range from periodic element manipulation to technological innovation. The level of scientific expertise and power can be as simple as mere experimental practices or as complex as altering the very laws of existence themselves.
