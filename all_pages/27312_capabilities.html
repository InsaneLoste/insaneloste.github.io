The user can gain powers through a symbiotic relationship with a meteorological/weather-based entity. Whether it is with a weather-controlling existence, a being of atmospheric force or an embodiment of the weather itself, the host’s relationship will afford them potent meteorological abilities. The powers can include elemental techniques, temperature control, and even pressure manipulation. Host and meteorological entity can benefit and/or suffer from each other’s state of being. As with most forms of Symbiosis, it can come with a price.
