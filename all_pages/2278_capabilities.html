Users of this ability can either mimic the traits of a Babaroga or are one, a Babaroga is a type of hag from Balkans folklore that acts much like a bogeyman figure via traveling from home to home performing feats of horrific child cruelty - sometimes she will simply scare them, other times sh will stuff them in a sack to eat them in her cave and in other cases she will abduct them via pulling them through tiny holes in the ceiling, resulting in a gruesome demise.
