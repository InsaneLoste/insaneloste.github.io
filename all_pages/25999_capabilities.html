A user of this ability either is or can transform into one of the Tuatha Dé Danann, a supernatural race from Irish mythology, typically depicted as gods and deities who were precursors to the Aos Sí, which were the most powerful type of faery. The Tuatha Dé Danann were portrayed as an opposite of the Fomorians, otherwise known as the Fomhóraigh, who were dark gods and opponents of the Tuatha Dé, with the Fomorians being gods who represented the destructive powers of nature, whereas the Tuatha Dé were representative of the helpful powers of nature. The Tuatha Dé Danann were thought to have been descended from the leader of the Nemed, the previous race that settled in Ireland. The Tuatha Dé had their homes in the Otherworld, a Celtic realm filled with deities and the dead.
