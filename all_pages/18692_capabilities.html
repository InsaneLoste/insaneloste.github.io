The user possesses their own vacuous effect/vacuum. They could possess a region lacking in energy and/or matter within their own body, an actual vacuum-like aspect and/or attachment or some type of entity/tool to which they are supernaturally connected. This could be biological, mystical or technological. Unlike Personal Void, one does not have to hold whatever they target; they simply exhibit attractive and/or repulsive effects. Whatever the origins, this power allows one the ability to manipulate vacuum abilities.
