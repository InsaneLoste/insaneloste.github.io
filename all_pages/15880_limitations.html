  • User's genetics may be unstable, resulting in side effects such as:
    • Accelerated Aging: The people who engineered the clone would give the clones a limited lifespan to prevent possible uprisings against their creator.
    • Infertility: The people who engineered the clone could engineer the clone's genetic with terminator genes, preventing the clone from procreating or even being cloned.
    • Emotionlessness: The clones can be engineered to possess no sense of human emotions and thus be nothing more than a biological weapon.
    • Self-Necrosis: The clone's physiology could be unstable to the point of death by breaking down via necrosis/decaying, causing them to painfully be dying out.
    • Multiple personalities: A clone might possess the personality of the person they were clones from as well as their personality traits at the same time.
  • May require Cryogenic Hibernation to suppress the negative effects of cloning/modifications.
  • Clones may be vulnerable to users of Behavioral Inhibition, which can be difficult or impossible to overcome without Free Will Manifestation.
    • In cases where they are controlled with a physical device, the device may have to be physical removed for them to be free.
