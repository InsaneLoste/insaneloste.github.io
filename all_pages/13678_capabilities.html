Users of this ability can either mimic the traits of a Kuko or are one, Kuko is the second and final stage of evolution for good-aligned kitsune and have obtained a state of power equal to those of the gods, however despite being supremely powerful they no longer actively engage in contact with the physical world and instead tend to act as a venerated elder within the grander cosmology (this is also why Tenko rank higher than them in good kitsune society, despite Kuko being stronger in terms of magical ability).
