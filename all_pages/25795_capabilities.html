The user with this ability either is or can transform into a Transcendent Vampire: a supremely powerful vampiric being with enough power to rival gods, or perhaps even being a vampiric god themselves. Transcendent Vampires are the supreme paragons of the species and are seen as the strongest of their race without equal. At times they are seen as the source of their race and the first of their kind, which leads to them being revered and worshiped by the entire vampire race. sometimes they either are or can ascend to the status of A God. others may even rise to be the absolute ruler of all vampires.
