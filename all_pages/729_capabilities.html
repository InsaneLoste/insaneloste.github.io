The user either is or can mimic/transform into an agropelter, an ape-like cryptid with long prehensile limbs. They dwell in the woods stretching from Oregon to Maine. The agropelter are territorial. They get extremely aggressive, especially towards lumberjack that invade their homeland. They have the arm strength to break of branches and throw them. Their arms can also be used as a whip. Their diet consists of mostly owls and woodpeckers.
