The user possesses absolute power over all existing angels, including themselves, and can freely bring new ones into existence. The power encompasses all kind of angels from regular angels, archangels, virtue angels, angel lords, tantric angels, cherubim angels, seraphim angels, powers angels, guardian angels, ruler angels, fallen angels, hell angels, sorcerer angels, nephilim even transcendent angels and so many more. They can be controlled, summoned, redefined, banished, erased and recreated at will, user's power over them transcending any potential resistance or limitation.
