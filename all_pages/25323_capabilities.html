The user can create, shape and manipulate thorns, hard, rigid extensions or modifications of leaves, roots, stems or buds with sharp, stiff ends. They can control the growth of thorns and naturally thorn-bearing plants at will. Furthermore, the user can cause naturally thorn-bearing plants to release their byproducts (petals, thorns, seeds, pollen, pheromones, toxins, etc.), or withhold them at will.
