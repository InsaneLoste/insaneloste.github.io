The user can utilize the Ordered Arts, a specialized system of techniques based around ordered power. This series of practices enables the user abilities/powers that are associated with order. One can have a natural access to the essence of order, have an order-based physiology or even be a living embodiment. Whatever the initial circumstance, engaging in the training allows one to manifest capabilities ranging from the personal (physical enhancement, regeneration, supernatural attacks, etc.) to the metaphysical (reality manipulation, universal law alteration, nigh omnipotence, etc.).
