A user with this ability can transform into a mage, either human or, if they are members of other species, a non-human, whether partially or completely, as well as use the abilities, traits and appearance/physiology of a mage by rearranging their own DNA structure or though magic itself. Users may be able to transform into a mage they have never encountered. They are able to transform into a mage that exist, alien mage and/or a mages that are extinct.
