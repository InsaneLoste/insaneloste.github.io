• Replications of powers are limited to the knowledge the user possesses about fictional and nonexistant characters.
  • Replication of multiple characters/powers may cause problems. Ex: mimicking the power of a character that is immune to poison while also mimicking the powers of a character that needs poison to live.
  • Inexperienced users may also possess character's personality and/or weaknesses.
  • User cannot summon, manipulate or create characters only mimic their powers.
  • User may not be able to mimic radically different physiology.
  • Cannot cosplay real people.
