The user can utilize the Bio-Energy Arts, a specialized system of techniques based around bio-energy power. Bio-energy is understood to exist in all forms of life. It can take many forms (chemical, electrical, heat, particle). While it is present, few know techniques that allow them to access these internal powers. For those who engage in unique exercises, they can learn to harness their bio-energy for a variety of purposes. They can augment their strength, speed and recuperative abilities. They can perform specialized attacks and defensive techniques. They can even learn to extend their biofield manipulation abilities beyond their own bodies and influence other types of energy and matter.
