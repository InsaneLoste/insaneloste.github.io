The user can create, shape, and manipulate vicious and hellish winds capable of twisting and destroying everything within their path. The user can use the demonic winds to savagely shred all within its gales or create massive tornadoes that annihilate everything with destructive power. In addition, the winds can also drastically affect the temperature of any area, making bitter cold winds that freeze everything or relentless burning winds that reduce everything to ashes.
