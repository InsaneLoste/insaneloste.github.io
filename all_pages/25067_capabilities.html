Users possess the traits, attributes, characteristics and/or capabilities of televisions (or TV), is a telecommunication medium for transmitting moving images and sound as well as a mass medium for advertising, entertainment, news, and sports. They achieve this either through full or partial transformation into televisions, choosing to mimic certain aspects of TVs, or simply already being a sentient television in the first place. In some cases, the user's transformed form is anatomically identical to their normal form, aside of being made of televisions, in which case it contains all to organs and is somewhat vulnerable to attacks. Alternately the user can transform into the homogeneous matter, without any part of their form being more important than the other.
