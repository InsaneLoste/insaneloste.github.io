• Cannot kill an Omnipotent Deity.
  • Cannot kill true immortals and Life & Death Transcendent users.
  • Killing a god may have profound consequences of the natural order within their domain. For example, if one were to kill a sea-god, the sea may become erratic and uncontrollable.
  • May need different skills/powers to kill some types of gods.
    • Demonic Slayer for divine demons.
    • Alien Slayer for aliens gods.
    • Monster Slayer for monster gods
    • Giant Slayer to giant gods
    • Angel Slayer to divine angels
    • Undead Slayer to undead gods
