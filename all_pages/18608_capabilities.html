The user possesses a personal patron, a supernatural benefactor (divine, angelic, demonic, spiritual, cosmic) that guides, protects and even enables them specialized abilities, information and/or equipment. The user may be a destined champion chosen to fulfill an archetypal role (hero, messenger, villain). They may be supplicants who have sought preternatural intervention. Whatever the circumstance, they are connected to guarantors who will provide them the power to accomplish desired tasks.
