The user can create, shape and manipulate precipitation, any product of the condensation of atmospheric water vapor that falls under gravity, including drizzle, rain, sleet, snow, graupel, ice pellets, and hail. Precipitation occurs when a local portion of the atmosphere becomes saturated with water vapor, so that the water condenses and "precipitates". Thus, fog and mist are not precipitation but suspensions, because the water vapor does not condense sufficiently to precipitate.
