The user can create a substituting replacement, wether in the form of an object or being, in the event of themselves, others or anything else being in danger or getting attacked. They can take any object or entity and switch places with them at the very last moment before any harm reaches them, usually as a means to catch enemies off guard. This is usually achieved either through the use of advanced speed to conceal their movements and seemingly disappear, creating the appearance of the user's position being displaced, replicating oneself, others and/or objects to use as decoys, transforming objects or other beings into replicates, remotely attracting or summoning items or entities to use as replacements or shields or by actually displacing a desired target along with the user.
