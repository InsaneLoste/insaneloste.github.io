The user has the spirit form of divinity and divine powers granting them an immense amount of raw power beyond the mundane, making the spirit divinity users divine beings by nature, a status which can be obtained several ways. Users can gain this type of divinity by having a divine spirit ancestry, being transformed into a divine spirit either by another more powerful spirit divinity, a deity, higher being, by faith, channels, acts as a vessel for, the power/soul of a deity, to mimic/steal powers from a divine spirit, be enhanced/evolved to the point of godhood, or just being a divine spirit in the first place. However, at times users of the divine spirit are weaker or stronger than deities depending on the type of divine being they are in question.
