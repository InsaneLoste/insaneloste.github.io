A user with this trait can take on the abilities of a Bannik or is one. A Bannik is the guardian spirit of a traditional bathhouse in Slavic folklore, such places were used much like saunas but were also places for childbirth and divination, thus they were seen as significant places of magic and tradition, Banniks would have their own special area and often forbade any Christian imagery within this area as they often invited spirits from the forests to join them in their baths.
