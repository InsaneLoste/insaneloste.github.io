The user can utilize the Spiritual Arts, a specialized system of techniques based around spiritual power and souls. Unlike magic, which is focused on sources of mystical power, spells or mana, these skills are based upon the manipulation of ethereal essence. These arts tend to be particularly personal as they often involve the user controlling their own soul. Through an understanding of the nature of this specialized essence, one can manifest various supernatural abilities ranging from combat skill to physical enhancement to supportive manifestations like healing.
