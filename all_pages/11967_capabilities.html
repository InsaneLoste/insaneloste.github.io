The user with this ability either is or can transform into a homunculus (plural "homunculi"), a type of artificial human connected to Alchemy, from the Latin for "little man". Their traditional depiction is as tiny people grown in jars, but they tend to differ a lot in the presentation. Homunculi generally act as a magician's assistant (a.k.a familiar), spy, messenger, or even assassin. They are capable of speech, though this is not always the case. If created correctly, they are loyal to a fault when it comes to their master.
