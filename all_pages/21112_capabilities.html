A user with this ability either is or can transform into a Red Dragon. This is a type of mythic Dragon associated with the power of blood, wounds and even death, a symbol that represents anger, aggression and danger. They can also embody the sun, masculinity, passion, energy, and even war or even the power of sin. They are, by far, one of the most feared Dragons as they can often be an omen of great danger and can be associated with demonic forces.
