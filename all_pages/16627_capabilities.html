The user can gain powers by through a symbiotic relationship with a natural/nature-based entity. Whether it is with a force of nature, a being of natural energy or an embodiment of the natural world itself, the host’s relationship will afford them potent abilities associated with the macrocosm. The types of abilities depend on the entity (organic, inorganic, geological, meteorological, etc.). Host and natural entity can benefit and/or suffer from each other’s state of being. As with most forms of Symbiosis, it can come with a price.
