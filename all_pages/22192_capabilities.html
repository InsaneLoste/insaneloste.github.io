The user can utilize the Sentient Arts, a specialized system of techniques based around sentient power. Practitioners are capable wielding unique abilities derived from intelligent sources of supernatural might. This type of power is special as its free will can not only synchronize with the user’s own natural desires but also offer unique vitality to enhance the wielder’s very being. These practices often require a deep level of trust and understanding as the living power may need to feel it can bond with the user confidently; however, once the user has gained a worthwhile connection with the intelligent essence, they will be able to exhibit various abilities based on its nature. The power can grow and evolve, allowing the user’s combat, defense and support abilities to do the same.
