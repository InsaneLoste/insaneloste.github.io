The user can manipulate their own means/methods of travel and transport. This includes personalized vehicles, fictional/imaginary means of travel, teleportation, moving surfaces, generated paths, portals, personal space-time, summoning/reverse summoning/banishment, world ejection, reality separation, and any/all other means of travel that can be conceived of. Users can even bestow themselves with new forms of travel, gain strength from traveling, and even change the very nature of travel in regards to themselves.
