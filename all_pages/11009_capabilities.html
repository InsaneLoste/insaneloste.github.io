The user can create, shape and manipulate any of the five elements of Godai, a fivefold conceptual scheme that many traditional Japanese fields that are derived from Buddhist dharma and traditional Chinese medical doctrine that traveled from China throughout east Asia to Japan. They are also a static or inert philosophical understanding of the traditional Japanese elements and study, similar to the Greek classical elements. The five main elements or building blocks are Earth (地 Chi (sometimes ji) or tsuchi), Water (水 Sui or mizu), Fire (火 Ka or hi), Wind (風 Fū or kaze), and Void or sky or heaven (空 Kū or sora).
