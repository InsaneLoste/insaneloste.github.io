The user can gain powers by hosting hair-based beings in one's body. Due to their nature, they are often situated in areas that are most often associated with follicles (top of the head, underarm, upper lip), though they can be attached anywhere. The entity can come in as many forms as there are hairstyles and be utilized according to the host's need, such as for hair-based attacks, defense and binding. As with other forms of Symbiosis, it can come with a price.
