User with this ability either is or can transform into a pixie (also pixy, pixi, pikzie, piksie and pigsie), a small humanoid creature in British folklore. Traditionally they are generally benign, mischievous, short of stature and attractively childlike; they are fond of dancing and gather outdoors in huge numbers to dance or sometimes wrestle, through the night, demonstrating parallels with folk celebrations originating in the medieval period.
