Users, either innately or through training, are masters of wielding dual katanas or daishō, a set of a katana and a wakizashi (or a tantō), with incredible expertise in speed, power, and skill, allowing them to perform feats such as stopping and deflecting bullets, decimating large objects or areas, cutting through a solid material such as steel, and even hold back their strength to deal damage to their foes without killing them. They also have greater versatility and advantage compared to conventional swordsman who uses one katana.
