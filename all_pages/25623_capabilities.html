The user can manipulate, generate and otherwise control traction, the force used to generate motion between a body and a tangential surface, and also the force which makes an object move over the surface by overcoming all the resisting forces like friction, normal loads, air resistance, rolling resistance, etc; and tension forces as well, which are described as the pulling forces transmitted axially by the means of a string, a cable, chain, or similar one-dimensional continuous object, or by each end of a rod, truss member, or similar three-dimensional object.
