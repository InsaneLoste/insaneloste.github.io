• Abilities that can kill the user instantly (i.e, Death Inducement, Disintegration, Destruction, etc.) or before the regeneration is completed can bypass the adaptations.
  • May be vulnerable to specific types of attacks.
  • Complete destruction of the body can kill the user.
  • The user may still feel pain.
  • One Hit Kill will kill the user before regeneration occurs.
  • Soul Removal and Temporal Erasure can kill the user.
  • Users of Healing Factor Nullification/Irreversible Destruction can prevent wounds from healing.
  • Users of Healing Erasure can remove this power.
  • As this level of regeneration depends on the functionality of the cells and/or molecules, those with abilities in association with radiation, can obliterate their healing factor to the point of ceasing functionality.
  • May still need to breathe, making Deoxygenation-based attacks effective.
  • Intelligence Manipulation and Reduction or Stupidity Inducement may nullify this ability's efficiency.
