Users of this ability can either mimic the traits of an Oshiroi Baba or are one. Oshiroi Baba are a type of yokai-hag that travel around during the hours of night looking for sake and makeup. Often, they will try and harass humans into giving them either of these things but are otherwise harmless creatures that do not do any true harm, largely because their appearance is so hideous humans naturally flee from them on sight.
