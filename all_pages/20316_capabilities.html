The user can create, shape and manipulate elastic energy force via power of mind. The utility of this capability is vast and undocumented; such a facet would enable the user to facilitate a great deal of incomprehensible feats. One can liquify warm bodies to sludge, treat ordinarily dense substances like silly putty, merge and elongate, reach and grasp via suffusing oneself to a medium, controlling the hardness or pliability of substances tactilely or remotely, etc.
