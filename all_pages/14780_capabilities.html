The user is able create, shape and manipulate elements that have magical properties. These elements, a form of esoteric essence created specifically from magic. As stated above, they possess arcane properties that grant them special advantages such as surpassing the limitations and weaknesses that their natural variations may possess. These elements may be linked to magical creatures/beings or magical forces. Because of their magical nature, these elements can be used for various magical purposes like healing wounds, summoning entities, creating a contract, imposing/breaking seals, etc. Advanced users can take it step further and create new elements either from scratch or as a working foundation of preexisting elements.
