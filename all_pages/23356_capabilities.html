The user can move, manipulate or otherwise interact with matter and energy by controlling the space it is occupying. As all matter and energy exist within the boundless three-dimensional space, by using the occupied space, the user can control the movement of the target matter and energy in a telekinetic manner. Dependent on the user, they may be limited to simpler but powerful feats such as lifting objects with tremendous force or more intricate procedures such as manipulating space at even subatomic scales, potentially rearranging the reality structure of the targets.
