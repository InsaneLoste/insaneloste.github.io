Users of this ability can take on the traits of a Jack-in-Irons or are one, a Jack-in-Irons is a type of malevolent "ghost-giant" found in Yorkshire Folklore and is depicted as a large, often hideous man covered in chains who haunts lonely roads - armed with a spiked club the Jack-in-Irons preys upon unwary travelers and animals, beating them to death and taking their heads, which the monster then proudly displays on his chained form.
