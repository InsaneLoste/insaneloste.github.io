The user can utilize the Team Arts, a specialized system of techniques based around synergistic power. These practices espouse a supernatural manifestation of the proverb, Unity is strength. While individuals can demonstrate powerful abilities through their own training, there are plenty of instances where those who work together are exponentially augmented. Combining their inherent or acquired abilities and techniques and synchronizing their essences (minds, energies, souls) allow for skills otherwise unavailable to lone practitioners. Whether they are enhanced attacks, heightened physical abilities or amplified preternatural phenomena, users become greater together than they ever could be alone.
