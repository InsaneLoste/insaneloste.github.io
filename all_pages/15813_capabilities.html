The user can gain powers through a symbiotic relationship with a miraculous/miracle performing being. The host can absorb the supernatural essence of an embodiment of miracles. Through this bond, the user can acquire abilities related to miracles and dark miracles. They can perform positive feats meant to heal, inspire and protect or feats meant to degrade, bewilder or destroy. With this bond, a user can cause amazingly unnatural or unexpected phenomena to manifest.
