Users can combine technology with eldritch like elements which portend from beyond realities edge. Even when it's wielders are well-versed in utilizing this technology to the point reverse-engineering it, it is never to be completely understood. That is to say, the technology is so alien or beyond in terms of fundamentals, physical or scientific laws and specifics that it is, for the lack of a better term, maddening to try to comprehend it without going without the cost of your sanity or soul.
