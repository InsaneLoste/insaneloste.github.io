• Soulless beings can still be granted a soul.
    • If user's soul was taken then Soul Restoration can counter this.
    • If users' soul was destroyed then Soul Recreation can counter this.
    • Users who can share soul can grant them to soulless beings.
  • May not be able to manipulate life force.
  • May be vulnerable to Possession.
  • Being soulless does not affect users of Eternal Bond.
  • Being soulless doesn't mean being mindless, so the user may be affected by a mind based abilities
  • Soul Creation
  • Not immune to Oblivion and Absolute Erasure.
  • In many verses the soul is an essential part of empathy, meaning that losing the soul (or having none to begin with) prevents a user from having true empathy - in essence they become psychopaths at best and demonic monsters at worst (in some verses even demons fear those truly without a soul, making them an almost eldritch being).
