The user can create, shape and manipulate holy twilight that is highly effective against the supernatural, such as demons and/or angels. They would be able to do all kinds of holy things with their unique type of element, which in question, can range from trapping and/or immobilizing to outright banishing or killing supernatural threats. Furthermore, divine twilight may have other properties and abilities, such as healing, resurrection, summoning angels and/or spirits of the deceased from Heaven to the material plane.
