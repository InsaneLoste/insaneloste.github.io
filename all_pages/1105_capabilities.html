The user is able use a form of magic that allows them to cast magical spells dealing with projectiles and similar forms of ammunition (e.g., bombs, missiles, warheads, landmines, naval mines, and anti-personnel mines), either magically creating them or infusing magic into preexisting ammunition. Because of their magical properties, not only can they do more damage and and travel farther than normal ammunition, they can also hurt most magical beings who are normally immune to normal ammunition.
