The user can utilize the Guardian Arts, a specialized system of powers/techniques based around guardian power. There are instances of individuals who possess unique supernatural abilities that have been pass down to one guardian to another. With each new generation becoming stronger through the achievements and progress of their predecessors, these powers/techniques can get stronger, adapt, and even evolve. Through practice and training, one is capable of exhibiting distinctive techniques based on their predecessors' abilities, including martial skills (attack powers), physical enhancements (heightened senses, speed, strength, etc.), supporting practices (healing) and/or skills affecting the nature of existence (universal force control, reality warping, etc.).
