The user with this ability either is or can transform into a vengeful ghost, a spirit/ghost who represents or is associated with vengeance, revenge, retribution, and even karma. They return from the afterlife to seek revenge for a cruel, unnatural or unjust death they went through, or even the case of the spirit's body not being buried properly, or their grave being disturbed. Vengeful ghosts were fairly common in mythology and greatly feared among civilizations.
