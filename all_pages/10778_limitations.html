• Because genius loci are spirits, they could be vulnerable to users of Soul Destruction, Soul Mutilation, Absolute Erasure, Absolute Destruction, and among other powers that can possibly affect, harm, and/or demolish souls/spirits.
  • Users of Purification to Omni Purification, can not only purify them, but this can demolish the tether binding them to the location/building, causing them to finally and peacefully, be escorted to the afterlife as opposed to banishing them outright.
  • If the genius loci in question was born from curses, blessings, or powerful magic, users of Blessing or Curse Removal, Magic Negation or Destruction can reverse the effects to the point of restoring the building/location.
  • Those of exorcism might be able to forcefully banish and remove the spirit from the location depending on how skilled they are.
