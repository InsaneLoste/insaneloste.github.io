The user can perform a form of magic related to homes, allowing for magical control over homes and/or their aspects and/or drawing on their mystical energies. The user can perform home magic; a form of magic that centers around the home as a source of power and energy, manipulating and harnessing the magical potential present within the very structure of a house. Users can cast spells related to home to manipulate everything within and outside to give user control over their home as anyway they see fit.
