The user can utilize the Transcendent Arts, a specialized system of techniques based around transcendent power. Different supernatural forces enable specialized abilities. These include magical, spiritual, elemental, psychic, extra-dimensional and many others. As potent as these are, there are upper levels of preternatural might one can potentially achieve. Through unique practices and training, one can gain godlike skills and abilities. This often involves a student pushing themselves beyond their worldly abilities. In doing so, one achieves a state of body, mind and/or soul that can manipulate aspects like universal essence, conceptual properties and even the fabric of reality.
