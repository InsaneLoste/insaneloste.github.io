• Axioms and principles rely on each other to function. Axioms would be meaningless without principles, and principles would be unable to form concepts without the structure and rules provided by axioms. As such, Principle Manipulation can make Axiom Manipulation useless, though the reverse is also true.
  • May only be able to affect specific systems or axioms.
  • Changing basic axioms of reality may damage or destroy the universe as we know it.
  • Changing axioms may or may not be retroactive.
  • May not be beneficial against Truth Manipulation, Meta-Concept Manipulation, Conceptual Transcendence, Meta Perspective Manipulation.
  • Almighty laws are far beyond axioms, and so axiom manipulation is useless against them.
