Users, either innately or through training, are masters of wielding and using chains or chain-like weaponry/tools, a serial assembly of connected pieces, called links, typically made of metal, with an overall character similar to that of a rope in that it is flexible and curved in compression but linear, rigid, and load-bearing in tension, beyond that of the peak members of their species. Users are typically able to demonstrate a nimble aptitude for utilizing chains as weapons. They can swing them with great skill in hand condition and can ensnare their opponents with no problem. They often possess serious dexterity and agility, as utilizing chains in combat requires a nimble and light body as well as hands able to wield it to max efficiency while not hurting themselves.
