User with this ability either is or can transform into a Koropokkuru, a race of small people in folklore of the Ainu people of the northern Japanese islands. The name is traditionally analyzed as a tripartite compound of kor or koro ("butterbur plant"), pok ("under, below"), and kur or kuru ("person") and interpreted to mean "people below the leaves of the butterbur plant" in the Ainu language.
