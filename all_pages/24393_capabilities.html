The user is able to produce supernatural/unnatural tears from their tear ducts that can have a variety of effects when they are triggered, the most common forms have sentient tear drops, with some users being able to heal, create or give life, poison, infect with a virus, simply bleed without being sick or dying, burn, freeze or even produce precious minerals with their tears like gemstones or gold, while others can have magical effects/properties when they touch other objects or individuals.
