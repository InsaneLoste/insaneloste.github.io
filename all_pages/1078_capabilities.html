Users of this ability are either a member of the Amanojaku or can mimic the traits of such, the Amanojaku are demons from Japanese folklore that appear as horrific little monsters similar to Oni or Yokai, they are extremely malevolent creatures that delight in not only harming and killing others but also corrupting people into performing evil deeds - for this purpose they are often found in opposition to the gods or other divine creatures, who often banish or imprison these vicious little creatures in order to ensure they can not do further harm.
