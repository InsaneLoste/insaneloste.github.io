The user can utilize the Imagination Arts, a specialized system of techniques based around imaginative power. Imagination fuels an individual’s creative process. This, in turn, allows one to produce various ideas, actions and physical constructs. Supernaturally speaking, there are instances of those who can wield their imagination to physically control reality or have it be the source of power for a particular set of preternatural capabilities. This is very potent; however, without training, one’s imagination or lack thereof, can lead to unintended or even dangerous consequences. By training in certain practices, one can learn to properly wield their imaginary powers to enhance their combat capabilities, heighten their defenses or manipulate the world around them.
