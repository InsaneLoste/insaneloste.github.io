The user can gain powers through a symbiotic relationship with an antithetical being. The symbionts (host and/or symbiote) are diametrically opposed to each other in terms of their natures; however, by linking with the symbiote, the host can acquire unique, supernatural powers beyond their individual capabilities. Due to the polarized, often chaotic relationship, this type of Symbiosis, more than others, can come with a price.
