• Magic Negation/Magic Destruction
  • Using/forcing spells/rituals without the blessings of their deities can have dire consequences.
  • Users magic may only be as strong as their deities their bound to.
  • Users magic may be powered by their faith
  • May summon something they cannot control.
  • May have to strike a deal with the deities in order to use their power and to keep using it.
  • May have to do the bidding of the deities or else they will lose some if not all of their power.
  • May not work if invoking false faith with false deities.
