The user, either innately or through training, is a master of selling/trading. They can perform feats such as locating any/all items for sale or trade regardless of how difficult (or impossible) it might be to find or sell and is always able to have the best or lowest price for said items and can convince others to buy them regardless of their level of value. Using this ability, the user can become wealthy very quickly and easily, and perform feats such as always making trades that generate profits, making the right choice when it comes to bartering and advancing said trade, and predicting the best time to make trades (buying/selling).
