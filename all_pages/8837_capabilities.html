The user can manipulate ephemerality, the conceptual nature of transience or temporary being. The whole of reality can be conceived as being made up of complementary aspects such as order and chaos, life and death, reality and illusion. One such set is permanence and ephemerality. Where permanence deals with the ability of tangible and intangible entities to endure the test of time and maintain themselves, ephemerality is concerned with the transitory nature of existence. There are various instances where material or immaterial things are known to only exist for a limited time. They come into being only under certain circumstances or are created to last for brief periods. Once conditions change or a particular purpose is served, they disappear or lose their current relevance.
