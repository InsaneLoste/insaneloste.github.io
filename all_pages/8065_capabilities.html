A user with this ability either is or can transform into an emotion deity, a deity associated with emotions, feelings, moods, sensations, empathy, passion, apathy and all their aspects. This grants them absolute knowledge and power over them, their concepts and principles. They are often worshipped by those who craved a specific emotion or sought to remove negative emotions, or possibly bless positive emotions upon their allies or curse their enemies with negative ones. Emotion deities weren't a common feature of worship in polytheistic religions but were important among ancient and current civilizations.
