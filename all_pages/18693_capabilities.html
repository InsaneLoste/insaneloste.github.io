The user can create, shape, and manipulate all the variables related to themselves, allowing them to define their current status, state of reality, definition, and overall existence. This will enable them to generate and change any variable connected with or to them, including space-time, attributes, properties, energy, probability, powers, weaknesses, strengths, body/biology, status, and even their existence. Changes to their variables may be either temporary or permanent, depending on one's skill and experience.
