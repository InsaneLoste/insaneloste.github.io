Users of this ability can either mimic the traits of a Ccoa or are one, a Ccoa is a type of divine beast found in the mythology of the Quechua people (who include a wide range of South American cultures, including the famous Inca) - in the contexts of the myths a Ccoa is a large feline with incredible powers, such as urinating rain, generating lightning from their eyes and causing both solar and lunar eclipses.
