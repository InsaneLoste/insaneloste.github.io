The user can gain powers by symbiotically hosting or linking to a living object. While there are instances where individuals possess a strong bond to certain inanimate items, in this type of connection, one is able to form a link to an object that is actually alive. Though it may exhibit aspects similar to those of an organic entity, the being is, at its core, a non-biological presence. It can be anything from an article of clothing to a weapon or even an accessory. Whatever the origins, the bond between living object and host can grant potent supernatural abilities based on its inherent characteristics, but as with most forms of Symbiosis, it can come with a price.
