Users of this ability can either mimic the traits of a Hinoenma or are one, Hinoenma are seductive female demons from Japanese folklore similar to succubi who pursue men, especially monks and clerics, tempting them into sinful relationships and draining them of both life and fortune, eventually killing them and moving onto a new victim shortly after. They are considered embodiments of feminine evil and seduction told as a caution on allowing carnal desires overcome the vows of a holy lifestyle.
