• Eternal Bond and Power Inheritance
    • The user cannot escape being the source of all death without passing it to another, as their must always be a Death.
  • If the user were to die or manage to be killed, all death in the cosmos would stop and the user would die/vanish. Mass immortality would run through the universe without end. However, no living thing would be able to heal from fatal wounds that should have killed the living in question, causing eternal pain.
  • Natural Order.
    • The user must obey the rules of the natural order of the cosmos and is rarely able to bend the rules outside of their power, unless under certain circumstances.
