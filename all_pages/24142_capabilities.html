User may spit with incredible accuracy and often consistency. Sometimes, range, impact strength and/or volume of material expelled are increased as well. This can be used to blind, stun or otherwise incapacitate a target. With larger volumes of material, the power can be used to cover large areas causing them to become slimy, sticky and/or slippery. Combined with other powers such as acid, toxin, or poison and you can have a devastating ability.
