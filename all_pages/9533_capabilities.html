Users, either innately or through training, are masters of wielding fans, any broad, flat surface that is waved back-and-forth to create an airflow. There are a wide range of fans from different cultures and races, all of each have varying effects when wielded with the prowess of a master. Most users are incredibly talented in utilizing it for aesthetic and performance purposes, like rituals and dancing, wielding fans with exceptional fluidity and beauty. They are also very familiar with the characteristics and attributes of fans, possessing extensive knowledge of them inside out. As a result, they are able to perform advanced actions and feats with fans, like expertly diverting air around fans to generate huge gusts of wind.
