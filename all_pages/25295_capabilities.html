The user can manipulate thermochemistry, the scientific field that deals with the relationships between chemical reactions, energy, and heat. When chemical reactions occur, there can be a temperature-based effect along with the creation of new substances. An exothermic reaction releases heat into the environment, creating a hot sensation. An endorthemic reaction absorbs heat, resulting in a cold sensation. These kinds of reactions play a big role in natural processes like calorie conversion and artificial processes like engine combustion.
