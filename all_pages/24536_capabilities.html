Users, either innately or through training, possess incredible mastery and ability in wielding a sword, a bladed melee weapon intended for cutting or thrusting, beyond that of the average person but in the realms of realistic skill and proficiency without nearing or reaching superhuman capabilities. They can also wield a sword with incredible expertise allowing them to perform feats like fight larger groups of opponents, deal damage to their foes without killing them, or hold their own in a conflict or against deadly opponents.
