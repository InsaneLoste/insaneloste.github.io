The user can utilize gender-specific arts, a specialized system of techniques and practices exclusive to one gender. These arts are completely unavailable to those of the opposite gender. Through practice and training, one is capable of exhibiting distinctive techniques based on their gender-specific abilities, including martial skills (attack powers), physical enhancements (heightened senses, speed, strength, etc.), supporting practices (healing) and/or skills affecting the nature of existence (universal force control, reality warping, etc.).
