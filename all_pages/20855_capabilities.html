The user can use radiosynthesis, the process of harnessing energy from ionizing radiation to empower one's metabolism or entire physique. This can allow users to fuel their activities. This ability can convert radioactive material from any location, such as a damaged nuclear reactor, fallout, an orphan source, or even background radiation. This grants the ability to boost one's metabolism to develop enhanced wellness and healthy cells, muscles, bones, and bodily systems. Another byproduct of this ability is that it allows the user to become resistant to high levels of ionizing radiation that could usually kill anyone. This will enable users to devour radiation efficiently.
