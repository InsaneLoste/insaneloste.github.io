The user of this ability is able to maintain, preserve and enforce the internal and external balance between two or more opposing forces in order the keep things in balance for the sake of the natural or cosmic order of the universe. Users are generally keepers of the balance and by choice are neutral but are still naturally empowered by two or multiple opposing forces that represent the balance of things and are in perfect balance with them, with the sole purpose keeping things at bay and in perfect harmony to preserve their respective worlds.
