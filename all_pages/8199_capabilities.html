User has the ability to put enchantments on something/someone; a type of spell that has power over others to alter/enhance the properties (magical, mundane, etc,) of things using magic from people, objects, or even spells by manipulating certain means of magic to permanently/temporary endow any kind of quality, ability, asset, etc, to others or themselves accomplish their goals which can come in a variety of spells from illusions, shapeshifting, bestowing and so on.
