Users with this ability have left behind their noble existence to become a much darker and corrupted being. The user embraces their new vile life, some might become deformed by their dark personality, emotions, action and/or dark powers they have embraced, sometimes minor such as skin color change or other bodily color change like their eyes or blood, or sometimes radical such as mutilation or transformation. They can even cause their disfigured, crippled or near-death selves to survive and become self-enabled again.
