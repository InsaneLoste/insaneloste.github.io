The user can cast anti-spells, specialized formulae, words or symbols that produce anti-magic effects. They can take the form of specific thoughts, gestures, rituals and other actions that can sometimes require additional components to function properly, such as the use of items like anti-magic objects or certain conditions such as celestial events. If the user is powerful or skilled enough, they can cast their anti-spells as nonverbal techniques, which do not require them to say the words needed to perform the anti-spell.
