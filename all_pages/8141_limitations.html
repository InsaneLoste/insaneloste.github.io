• Useless if target(s) are deaf or are using a non-verbal form of communication.
  • Does not grant multilingualism. As such, this ability can be defeated if the target cannot or does not speak a language that the user understands.
  • Weak users may not be able to hear through their own ears while using another's. As such, they can be snuck up upon.
  • May require focus to use.
  • May need to see/hear the target. May need to be within range of the target.
  • Use of target's auditory powers may cause the user headaches, unless the user shares those powers.
