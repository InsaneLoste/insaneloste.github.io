The user has the ability to manipulate the abyss, a realm that is home to souls who go there when they die. It is an eternal void to endure absolute isolation from the everything in existence. For that reason, there are those who would call it a fate worse than hell itself. Here, the souls that dwell there are forever doomed to wallow alone without any love or peace, becoming nothing as they lose pieces and bits of who they may be to the absolute emptiness.
