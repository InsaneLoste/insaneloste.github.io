• Lower fairies are unable to possess this ability.
  • Users are unable to affect other types of beings with this ability outside fairies themselves.
  • Users of this great power may have absolute control over others of their own kind; however, they can be temporally overthrown by several major fairies at once if caught off guard. This is only temporary as these fairies or beings of this power are naturally more powerful.
  • Some users of this power cannot be killed because it could take any created fairies by them with them to the grave, effectively killing them as well. This may not always be the case.
  • Transcendent Fairies are not affected by this power as it is an innate power of their very being.
