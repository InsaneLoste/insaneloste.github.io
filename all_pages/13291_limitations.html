• Absolute Restoration can restore any damage dealt by the user.
  • Though the user can still deal out irreversible damage, they are still vulnerable to damage themselves.
    • Thus if the user ends up dealing a powerful attack to themselves, they may never be able to heal the damage.
  • The damage may be irreversible, but the degree is dependent on the user, so any weak attacks may make the ability useless.
  • Event Negation might cause the attack to never have happened.
  • Users of Omnilock, Meta Power Immunity, and Absolute Immortality are immune.
  • Users of Flawless Restoration and Absolute Regeneration are immune.
