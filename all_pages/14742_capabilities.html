The user can magically scan anyone/anything and analyze the data and information that is gathered. This power can either be an innate skill or through the use of a magical item. They can use this to determine the intimate properties, detect the presence, learn the skills and ability of others, and diagnose the condition of whoever/whatever they scan, and find any/all inconsistencies/errors. the user would usually have an inner being to act as a living archive to help the user.
