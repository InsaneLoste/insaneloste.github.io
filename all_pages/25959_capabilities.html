Users of this ability can either mimic the traits of a Trotterkopf or are one, a Trotterkopf is a type of familiar found in Pennsylvania Dutch folklore and resembles in many ways a goblin - they serve witches and other evil magic-users, traveling by hours of night to do their bidding, sadly not many specifics are known but a great many prayers and rituals seemed to be created specifically to ward off these creatures, showing they were a large part of life for the superstitious.
