• Bloodlessness
  • Distance, mass, precision, etc. depend upon of the knowledge, skill, and strength of the user, and their power's natural limits.
  • May be unable to create blood, being limited to manipulating only from already existing sources.
  • May be limited to manipulating the blood of others or only themselves.
  • May bleed to death by drawing too much blood.
  • Risk of developing Thrombosis when manipulating blood.
  • Some users could be limited on what type of blood they can control, e.g. animal blood.
  • To manipulate one's own blood for external applications, one may have to draw blood.
