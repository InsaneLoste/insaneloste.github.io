The user can gain powers by symbiotically hosting or linking to a volatile being. The entity could be a being of living, detonation force, an embodiment of volatility or an existence that controls violent energetic release. Whatever its origins, by linking to the entity, the host acquires powers related to explosions, implosions, pyrotechnics, etc. Due to the unstable and destructive nature of the bond, this form of Symbiosis can come with a price.
