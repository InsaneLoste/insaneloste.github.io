• Users of Logic Immunity are immune.
  • The power being illogical by nature, the writer could reasonably come up with illogical limitations.
  • Can cause unintended alterations to reality.
  • Cannot affect concepts that are beyond logic.
    • Absolutes and abilities derived from them exist entirely above logic, so this power is irrelevant to them.
    • Almighty Laws may still bind the user.
    • Higher Dimensions/Planes of Existence may operate off of 'higher-logic', which is beyond the scope of a normal logic manipulator. Even alteration of one's personal logic would be ineffective, as the higher logic overrules it completely.
