The user can create, shape and manipulate metal derived from the depths of Hell, sporting the capability to destroy nearly anything it strikes with absolute malevolent intent. In addition, demonic metal is capable of inflicting excruciating pain, both physically and spiritually tormenting the victim. It has the potential to corrupt one's soul, and is very effective against the holiest of supernaturals, such as angels. More powerful users can even ultimately summon/resurrect/create evil spirits, elemental entities and/or demons from Hell.
