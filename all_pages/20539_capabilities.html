Users can utilize the Purification Arts, a specialized system of techniques based around purified power. There are various instances of individuals who can wield powerful forces that can grant them supernatural abilities, but are also defined by their cleansed natures. These essences can cause potent healing and renewal to the targets and the environment, sanitize various entities (either purging contamination or vanquishing them altogether) or even create beneficial effects on the user, augmenting their being to an ascended state. They often become lighter, refined or even divine versions of themselves by wielding such power. Learning to wield purified essences can grant one great power such as enhanced physiology, access to redemptive energies/matter or even the power to alter the nature of reality in a reformed way; however, one must be careful not to be overtaken by this power, as its inherent quality is one of decontamination.
