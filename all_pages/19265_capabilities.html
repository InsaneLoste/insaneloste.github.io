A user with this ability either is or can transform into a Pleasure Deity, a god/goddess who represents and is associated with pleasure, enjoyment, delight, cheer, joy, mirth, laughter and ecstasy, and all their aspects. This grants them absolute knowledge and power over them, their concepts and principles. Pleasure Deities weren't a very common feature of worship in polytheistic religions, however they can be found in most of recorded history in various forms.
