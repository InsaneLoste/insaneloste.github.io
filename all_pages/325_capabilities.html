User's bodily systems grow to be stronger, tougher, faster and better at a much faster rate as the body grows. Unlike Accelerated Body, which is just limited to bodily speed, the user's body systems, all of them, grow over time as the user ages/matures, making them better than those of other people. Their nervous system, skeletal system, digestive system, immune system, muscular system, etc. all grow better and faster as time goes by.
