The user has the "Demonic" form of Divinity, and divine powers granting them an immense amount of raw power beyond the mundane, making the users divine demonic beings by nature and a dark divine being, a status which can be obtained by several different ways. Users can gain this type of divinity by having an dark divine ancestry (being born a demon-deity), being transformed into an divine demon either by another more powerful divine demon, a deity, higher being such as a transcendent demon, ascended transcendent demon, etc, users have dominion and godhood in the divine and demonic aspects which makes them formidable in both worlds capable of overcoming all manner of demonic entities by sheer will alone. However, at times users of the demonic divinities are more powerful or even weaker than other deities depending on the type of divine being they are in question, some may even equal original dark divinities due to both being connected to darker forces.
