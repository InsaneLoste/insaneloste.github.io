Users use magical symbols to create a quasar objects which sizes depends on the power of the user. Quasar magic mix the dark energy of a black hole and the heat of the billions of neutron stars in one magical blow. Quasars are cosmic objects, generally active-nucleus galaxies, which shoots rays of matter from their black holes. Quasar magic also involves mixing quasars to enhance the power of the attack. It is mainly used by cosmic entities as a way to push enemies while setting them on cosmic fire and dealing a great amount of damage.
