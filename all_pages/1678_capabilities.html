A user with this ability either is or can transform into an Archery Deity, a god/goddess who represents or is associated with archery, bows, arrows, accuracy, and marksmanship. Archery Deities were common in mythology and were important among ancient and current civilizations. They have been a part of great wars for the welfare of the universe they are in. With their skills in archery, they have slayed thousands of extremely powerful demons.
