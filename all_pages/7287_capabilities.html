Users can create, shape, and manipulate dust, fine particles of matter from various sources, such as soil dust lifted by weather (an aeolian process), volcanic eruptions, and pollution. Dust in homes, offices, and other human environments contains small amounts of plant pollen, human and animal hair; textile fibers; paper fibers; minerals from outdoor soil; human skin cells; burnt meteorite particles; and many other materials which may be found in the local environment.
