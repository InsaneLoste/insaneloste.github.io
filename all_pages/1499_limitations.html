• Arcanepotence/Magic Ascendancy/Meta Magic/Mystic Lock
  • May require outside source of anti-spells to make defensive maneuvers.
  • Users will be exhausted when too much energy is used.
  • May be over-charged/wounded if too much energy is used at once.
  • Can only be used for defensive purposes.
  • Improperly cast/created anti-spells may result in undesirable effects or no effects at all.
