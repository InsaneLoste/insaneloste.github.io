The user is able to manipulate their own personal yin and yang essences. Just as is the case with the cosmos at large, within all beings exist the complementary forces of yin and yang. The user is special in that they can actually tap into the specialized capabilities associated with each of the powers inside of them separately. This grants them powers related to yin (darkness, cold, negativity, femininity, etc.) and yang (light, heat, positivity, masculinity, etc.).
