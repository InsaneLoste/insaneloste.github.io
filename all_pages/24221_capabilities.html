User can copy and combine the best traits and aspects of several things to get the best out of all of them without flaws on themselves, others, or create something new with the aspects and traits, whether lifeform, style/type, etc. They could combine/copy the strength of a weightlifter, the speed of a track runner, the agility of an acrobat, and the skill of a martial artist, combine the best of every martial arts to create the ultimate martial art or combine several super-powers into one.
