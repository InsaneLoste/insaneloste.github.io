The user can create, shape and manipulate matter of draconic origin. They would be able to do all kinds of things with their unique type of substance and special properties for various purposes, such as enhancing their physique, healing, teleportation, etc., even ranging from creating blasts to creating or summoning beings of draconic matter. Users would even be able to summon dragons themselves; because of its draconic nature, it is made so that no one is capable of using this matter except for those who are worthy of it.
