Users of this ability have the ability to take on the traits of a Mazikeen or are one, a Mazikeen is an invisible demon from Jewish folklore that was said to be capable of both small-scale mischief and larger destruction, while originally unseen more modern versions of the creature seems to favor them as winged female demons similar to succubus, however this is a purely modern addition as the original lore depicts them more as unseen malicious spirits that one may encounter in daily life.
