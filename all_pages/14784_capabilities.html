User can create, shape, absorb or manipulate magical energy; a form of mystical energy that fundamentally fuels and/or manifests all magical phenomena such as spells, practices, rituals, circles and enchanted objects. through this mystic energy users can transmute magic for a variety of effects and purposes, including obtaining magical powers from external and internal source, yielding upstanding results in the users magical practices. Users can even invoke other forms of energies for specific forms of magic that require said necessary energies to empower their spells.
