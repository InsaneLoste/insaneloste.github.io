The user can create, shape and manipulate hellish music, capable of deafening, blasting away and destroying anyone or anything in its path. In addition, demonic music is capable of inflicting excruciating pain, both physically and spiritually tormenting the victim. It has the potential to corrupt one's soul, and is very effective against the holiest of supernaturals, such as angels. More powerful users can even ultimately summon/resurrect/create evil spirits, elemental entities and/or demons from Hell. Potentially, the music can hypnotize victims or alter any nearby aspect of reality.
