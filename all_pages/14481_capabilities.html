The user possesses powers and abilities that are Lovecraftian/eldritch in nature. This definition includes powers that are evil by nature, are unique or abnormal, gained by unpleasant circumstances, produce side effects such as mutations, generally hard to control, and are clearly unnatural. Usually, when you gain some special ability, it will manifest in some fairly conventional forms. Energy blasts, Psychic Powers, steel skin. Oftentimes, the user's power centers on forms of body horror, such as tentacles, melting flesh, multiple limbs/eyeballs and cancerous growths and then there are Lovecraftian superpowers.
