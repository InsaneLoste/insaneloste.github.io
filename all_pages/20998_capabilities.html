The user can utilize the Reality Arts, a specialized system of techniques based around existential power. Controlling the fabric of reality allows one nigh-omnipotent capabilities; however, without proper focus and training, this kind of power can potentially lead to devastating effects that can be as limited as affecting only the user to influencing an entire planet or universe. By engaging in specialized practices, one can learn to hone this type of power to properly exercise specific techniques. Due to the inherent nature of reality warping, the list of skills to be be gained is possibly limitless, including but not confined to physical augmentation, energy and/or matter manipulation, healing, regeneration and controlling conceptual aspects like creation and destruction.
