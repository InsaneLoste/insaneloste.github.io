Users of this ability can either mimic the traits of a Nomori or are one. Nomori are large and powerful yokai that resemble serpents but also have six arms, each ending in powerful gripping talons. Nomori are predators just like any other snake but also act as guardians of nature, which puts them into conflict with humans. Though they often live far away from most human habitats, and while dangerous, if confronted are more neutral in regards to people, providing they are left alone and nature is not disturbed.
