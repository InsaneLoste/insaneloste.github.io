The user has the titans/giants form of dark divinity and divine powers granting them an immense amount of raw power beyond the mundane, making the giant divinity user's divine beings by nature, a status which can be obtained several ways. Users can gain this type of divinity by having a giant ancestry, being transformed into a titan/giant either by another more powerful giant divinity, a deity, higher being, by faith, channels, acts as a vessel for, the power/soul of a deity, to mimic/steal powers from a divine giant, or be enhanced/evolved to the point of godhood. However, at times users of the divine giant are weaker or stronger than deities depending on the type of divine being they are in question.
