• Unlike many other mythical physiology-based powers the existence of a Todaiki provides no benefit to the user, instead it traps them in eternal servitude to another as well as eternal torment - as such it is likely one of the more horrific fates to befall a person in its native folklore.
  • While seemingly impossible to escape from a fully omnipotent being or sufficiently powerful deity could likely free a person from this existence.
  • Requires one to be captured and changed via dark arts, so beings who are resistant or immune to magic would be completely unharmed.
