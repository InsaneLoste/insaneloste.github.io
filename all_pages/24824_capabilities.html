The user is able to create living beings through the manipulation of technology. Whether they form the basis of their physiology or are the animating/creative force, technology/mechanical devices are used to create newfound animated existences. They can come with sentience and even souls when created. Unlike Mechanical Creature Creation, this power allows for actual life to come into being, not simply technologically-based forms. The life creation aspect also makes it so the entities do not need to be made of technology; they can simply be powered by them.
