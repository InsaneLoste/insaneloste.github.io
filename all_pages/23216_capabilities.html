Users embody space, having natural affinity and physiology of the universal force. More advanced users have infinite and all abilities and powers based on space. The user can even teleport any sort of matter, object or beings from one location to another. They can also create portals to another dimension. Being the embodiment of space, one can be anywhere and everywhere at once. At the pinnacle, users are all-powerful over the three dimensions of space, possessing absolute and limitless control and immunity of space, doing absolutely anything with it.
