The user possesses their own, highly personalized equipment. They may be able to create personal/special equipment (like weapons, armor, vehicle, etc.) to achieve a particular objective. With this, the user is prepared for any kind of situation. The equipment can only be utilized by the user; they possess a symbiotic and empathic relationship with the equipment. It can sense their intentions through semi or complete conscious thought, making the user capable of manipulating the equipment and gain special powers from it.
