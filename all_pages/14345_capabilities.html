The user can utilize the Literary Arts, a specialized system of techniques based around literature-based power. Literature can carry great force beyond the ability to inform and inspire. Books, cards, comics, scripts, scrolls, symbols and the like can possess incredible supernatural might provided one learns how to properly handle them. One of the most common forms of practice is using magic books or grimoires. While these kinds of materials have the potential to grant almost anyone great power who uses them, there are those who undergo training to access abilities that are only available to a few. Sometimes, it is a matter of an item having an idiosyncratic nature, being bonded to a particular person; in other cases, one must perform specialized tasks to gain unique power. This can be the case with other types of compositions and inscriptions.
