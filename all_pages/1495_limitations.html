• May need to perform certain actions/fulfill conditions to use anti-magic, ranging from specific words/songs, inscription/incantation, certain hand/body moves, materials that may be consumed or even extensive rituals.
  • Incorrect pronunciation and word choice of an anti-spell may have negative effects (such as the anti-spell being thrown back at the user, producing a different effect than expected or part of reality may fall apart).
  • May need to use special artifacts such as books, herbs, and talismans.
  • May be limited on how many anti-spells they can cast.
  • May require concentration to work.
  • Certain anti-spells may only work for as long as the caster is alive.
  • Certain anti-spells may be limited to a specific group of anti-spell casters.
  • Certain anti-spells may have a negative effect on nature and require a balance to the universe.
  • All Anti-Spells Require Arcane Power to fuel their anti-magic, such as Ambient Energy (Ley Lines, Environmental Essence, Otherworldly Power), power from other mystical beings (Gods, Angels, Demons, Dragons, Eldritch Abominations) or other sources of esoteric energy.
    • More powerful anti-spells may require more anti-magic power and training to work.
      • Untrained novices can easily misuse an anti-spell, creating unpredictable results.
  • Some anti-spells may taint the user's soul and turn them down the path of evil/madness/disorder, and therefore, are best left unknown.
  • Some anti-spells may simply need to be spoken in reverse to be negated.
  • Once an anti-spell is casted, it may not be able to be altered.
  • Overuse may lead to physical and/or mental damage, temporary/permanent loss of power or death.
