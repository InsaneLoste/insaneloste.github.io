Users with this trait can take on the abilities of a Koromodako or are one, a Koromodako is a supernatural octopus from Japanese folklore that only grow to a few centimeters long but hide a vicious secret, if angered the Koromodako can rapidly expand its size to unimaginable scales and engulf anything within itself, including entire ships - it then drags its victims to a watery demise, devours everything still in its grasp and then shrinks back down to its usual tiny form to once again float aimlessly in the ocean until it is angered again, repeating the process indefinitely.
