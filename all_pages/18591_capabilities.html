The user possesses their own personal mount, an entity (usually an animal) that is used as their main form of transportation. This often involves a supernatural bond between rider and steed, allowing them to synchronize and perform various supernatural techniques. The rider may be able to communicate with their mount, summon them from far distances or even be able to merge with them, gaining a symbiotic link that allows them to wield specialized powers. Whatever the circumstance, the rider becomes more powerful through their preternatural bond with their steed, if only through the acquisition of greater mobility.
