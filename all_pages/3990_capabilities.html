The user is able to manipulate the chakra points, lotus-shaped, internal wells of esoteric energy that flow through the human body according to Hindu and Buddhist beliefs. Beginning at the base of the spine and ending at just above the head, these vortices, each associated with its own color and rotational speed, are said to not only connect with various types of supernatural essence, but also have specific effects on different health conditions and emotions when developed and opened. Various traditions teach of different numbers of energy centers in the body. Although the highest number listed is 114, most are familiar with a seven-chakra system.
