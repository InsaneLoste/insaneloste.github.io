• Lower monsters are unable to possess this ability.
  • Users are unable to affect other types of beings with this ability outside monsters themselves.
  • Users of this great power may have absolute control over others of their own kind, however, they can be temporally overthrown by several major monsters at once if caught off guard. This is only temporary as monsters or beings of this power are naturally more powerful.
  • Some users of this power cannot be killed because it could take any created monsters by them with them to the grave, effectively killing them as well. This may not always be the case.
