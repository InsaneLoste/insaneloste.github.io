• Beings that are neither worthy nor unworthy may be immune to the enchantment:
    • Alien/Fathomless Mind
    • Morality Transcendence
    • Non-sentient beings (e.g., artificial intelligences).
    • Soullessness
  • Controlling the enchantment:
    • Enchantment may lock out its creator if they're not worthy.
    • If the controller is exempt, this may create a "back door" that others can exploit.
    • Moral Manipulation, or Morality Manipulation of the controller, may change the enchantment's definition of "worthy".
  • Using the object:
    • May take years before a worthy wielder appears.
    • Something may happen to make the wielder unworthy.
    • Usurp Worth can bypass the worthiness requirement.
  • Worthiness Bypassing
