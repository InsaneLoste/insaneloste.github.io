The user is able to deal extreme damage with their attacks. This can be done with an effective, a precise strike to an opponent's body, and/or with a special ability that happens to be the enemy's weakness. When a user of this power gets a critical hit, they may deal twice the damage, induce severe pain, instantly incapacitate or knock out the enemy, or instantly destroy/kill the target. Highly advanced users can take down their opponent with one attack.
