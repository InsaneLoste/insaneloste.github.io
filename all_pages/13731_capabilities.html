A user with this ability either is or can transform into a Land Nymph. They are nymphs and minor goddesses associated with and presiding the lands and it's landscapes. There are five different types of Land Nymphs in mythos, the Alseids, the Auloniads, the Orestiads, the Leimakids and the Napaeae. All possessing the same powers with slightly different functions over the lands and it's areas.
