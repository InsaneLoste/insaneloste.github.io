The user can charge a target(s) with electricity, whether they be an object or a person. The magnitude of which can range from a small zap, to the equivalent of a major city's power grid. With this in mind, the user can use their ability to get electronics working by recharging them, restart hearts like defibrillators or fry entire networks. Users can even overcharge their subjects, giving them a massive power boost. Conversely, if their target is not built to store electricity or simply cannot keep up with amount infused, the consequences can be fatal and destructive.
