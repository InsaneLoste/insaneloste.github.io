The character is “the Slender Man”, an anomalous humanoid with various nebulously defined characteristics and abilities. The most defined characteristics of a Slender Entity is that of an exaggerated height, abnormally thin stature, pale skin and long spindly limbs, as well a tendency to stalk people and induce great fear and other adverse effects with their presence alone. Slender Men are well known for having supernatural abilities like elasticity, mind manipulation, photographic existence, size shifting, teleportation and tentacles.
