The user can gain powers through a symbiotic relationship with a death-force/death-based being (such as an embodiment of death-force or an entity that controls and/or is comprised of it). Thanks to its necrotic essence, the bond between host and entity can impart the power to control death, decay and even souls. Because of the inherently lethal nature of the power being wielded, this type of Symbiosis can come with a price.
