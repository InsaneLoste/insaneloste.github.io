The user can create, shape and manipulate ionizing radiation. They are generated when radiation has energy to liberate electrons from atoms or molecules, thereby ionizing them. Radioactive isotopes such as gamma rays, x-rays, and higher-tier ultraviolet rays are considered forms of ionizing radiation. Due to the unstable nature of ionic rays, ionizing radiation can have many negative effects on living organisms, causing things like cancer, radiation sickness, cell damage, radiation burns, and even death.
