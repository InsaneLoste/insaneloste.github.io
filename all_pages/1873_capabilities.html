The user can gain powers by symbiotically hosting or linking to an artificial and/or synthetic force being. The entity can be a being that is connected to a particular artificial force or operates as a synthetically created being. It could even be an entity that wields real incarnations of specific power. Whatever its nature, bonding with said figure allows the host access to fabricated versions of supernatural forces. These powers run the gamut from being inferior, comparable or superior to the traditional powers they emulate. Just as with most forms of Symbiosis, it can come with a price.
