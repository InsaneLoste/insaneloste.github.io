The user has control of anything that is symbolized as both godly and demonic, and has connections to gods and demons. They are able to manipulate sacred and profane artifacts and relics that are connected to the gods, demons, or their power. Due to controlling both godly amd demonic, the user could be in complete balance between the two forces. Additionally, this ability not only grants the user great strength, abilities, and control over numerous variations of godly-demonic powers, but also allows them to overwhelm demons and angels as well as rival that of powerful entities.
