The user of this ability is a mummy of superior power. Much like the rest of the empowered races, super mummies are much stronger than regular mummies, and they tend to be more difficult to dispatch, at least depending on what powers they possess. They tend to be much more intelligent than regular zombies, though some superior zombies can be described as nothing more than an unthinking juggernaut. Many greater mummy were praised as pharaohs and queens or their influential advisors/consorts in life.
