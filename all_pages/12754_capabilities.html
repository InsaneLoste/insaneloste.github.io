User can create, shape and manipulate a form of energy that attacks the body like an infection, making them contagious with diseases, viruses, plagues, etc.,. The infection then spreads to any other potential hosts in proximity of the target, inflicting similar symptoms on them. The cycle then repeats itself, spreading the infection to other potential hosts. The reach of the infection can range from a small group of people to an entire city to an entire continent to the entire world, etc.,.
