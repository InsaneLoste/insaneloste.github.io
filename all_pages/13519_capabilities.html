A user with this ability either is or can transform into a Kijimuna, a wood spirit native to the island of Okinawa. They're depicted as a child-sized humanoid creatures covered in red hair and large heads. They are said to live in trees, commonly the banyan trees. They are known to pull pranks on humans. Despite their mischievous nature, they've been known to make friends with humans until it goes sour. They are also skilled fishermen that catch many fish, only to eat one of their eyes.
