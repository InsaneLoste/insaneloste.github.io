The user can create, manipulate, and destroy wraiths, ghastly spirits of the darker realms, ordering them to lend aid in various day to day odds and ends, be it for battle, scouting out locations for items and intel or even information about a particular subjects, use them for personal and/or other benefits useful to the user and others. These tamed poltergeists may even cultivate bonds with the user, thus creating friendships. Users also may empathize or communicate with them.
