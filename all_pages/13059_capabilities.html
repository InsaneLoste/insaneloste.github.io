The user can instill and infuse vast amounts of knowledge into their mind by various means (supernatural, technological, etc.), rather than acquiring it through normal means such as study, experience, etc. The knowledge can be of any and all kinds, natural, supernatural, the known, the unknown, etc. One with this ability may have instilled knowledge in a specific field of study (like math, literature or a branch of science) or all subjects. The user may even have knowledge about the past, present or future, about alternate realities, the minds of others and knowledge beyond normal comprehension.
