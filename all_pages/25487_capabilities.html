The user gains the traits of a toilet demon, a malevolent spirit from numerous world cultures that were believed to exclusively haunt bathrooms, often bringing misfortune and death to any they came across - they are the direct opposite of a toilet deity in that they bring forth disease and other small-scale calamities, it may fall to a toilet deity to help ensure such evil spirits do not inhabit the area but often it also falls upon mortals to keep these spirits at bay (for example by ensuring good hygiene).
