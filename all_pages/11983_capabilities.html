The user is a master of wielding hooks, tools consisting of a length of material, typically metal, that contains a portion that is curved or indented, such that it can be used to grab onto, connect, or otherwise attach itself onto another object. Normally, users would be able to perform great feats and tasks with hooks, for almost any purpose, be it harvesting, exploring, parkouring or combat. In terms of combat use, they are able to wield them with great skill and brute power in both close and ranged combat situations, being able to use it for a myriad of purposes. For example, using hooks to pull enemies within striking distance and using a combo with another weapon or pierce enemies with sharp hooks. Some users may opt to use variants of hooks, like fishing hooks, chained hooks or grappling hooks. More advanced users would harbor extensive knowledge of hooks, knowing them inside out or being able to wield and utilize esoteric or supernatural variants of hooks as well.
