Users embody curses or the cursed, possessing key physical characteristics and/or personality traits related to it. Most of them having a natural and innate affinity to the cursed as well as curses in general, being immune to any and all curses. They possess cursed and curse-based abilities superior to any other being due to personifying the concept itself, usually casting immense and powerful misfortune on others, or even warping reality and the very foundation of existence as a result. More advanced curse embodiments are the prime source of all curses and have absolute dominion over any and all forms of it.
