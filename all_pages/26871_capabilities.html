A user with this ability either is or can transform into a Virtue Deity, a deity associated with Virtues, Purity, Righteousness, Honor, Chivalry, Glory, Respect, Discipline, Morality, Caution, Charity, Chastity, Compassion, Humility, Diligence, Patience, Temperance and all their aspects.  This grants them absolute knowledge and power over them, their concepts and principles.  They are often worshiped by those who strove to be virtuous and those who sought to make themselves pure in a spiritual sense.  Virtue Deities weren't a common feature of worship in polytheistic religions but were important among ancient and current civilizations.
