Users possess an inner core, an internal source that deeply intertwines within their very essence, serving as the quintessential power source for their exceptional abilities. This faculty of energy and latent potential lies dormant within them, waiting to be awakened and harnessed. It can manifest in various forms – an inner entity, an inner force, or even an entire inner world – but its purpose remains constant: to empower individuals with a unique and intrinsic connection to an extraordinary capabilities that is woven into their distinct attributes and nature.
