The user can utilize the natural arts, a specialized system of techniques based around nature. The user is able to wield a skill set that is focused on the cultivation and manipulation of ecological forces (animals, plants, elements, land, weather, etc.). The individual may have a nature-based physiology, possess an innate connection to ecological energy or be a living force of nature. Whatever the origins, the training allows one to wield it in a variety of ways that are usually based upon ecological power. This can include physical power augmentation, matter/energy manipulation and heightened rapport with the macrocosm.
