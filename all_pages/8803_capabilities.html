The user can utilize the Environmental Arts, a specialized system of techniques based around environmental power. Various environments are characterized by the entities and elemental forces that exist within them. Through specialized training, one can acquire supernatural abilities based on the specific territories with which they connect. They can alter the inherent nature of the environment they inhabit. They can draw power from the locale to enhance their own abilities. They can take control of the different creatures and materials that comprise them. Potentially, one could even learn to become one with the surrounding area. It is often limited to one specific type of environment; however, with further training, one could learn to become a force of great influence over all types of environments.
