The user can manipulate a catastrophic and potent variation of teleportation, which warps, rends, tears, breaks or otherwise mars anything unfortunate enough to be moved by this power. The user can teleport matter and energy, but leaving them damaged, injured, or demolished, unleash powerful teleportation waves that randomly teleport anything while leaving overwhelming spatial pressure in the user's surroundings, or more simply may teleport in a "piece-meal/incomplete" ways, e.g. teleporting the victim in order of skin, flesh, bones & then organs, if not just ripping chunks out of the target.
