Users of this ability can either mimic the traits of a Pugot Mamu or are one, a Pugot Mamu is an evil version of the more famous Pugot spirits of Filipino folklore and mythology, while both resemble large humanoids with no head and a large mouth full of sharp fangs a Pugot Mamu has evolved into a horrific man-eater that craves the flesh of children: in contrast the Pugot is a neutral being that feeds on snakes and chickens, never humans.
