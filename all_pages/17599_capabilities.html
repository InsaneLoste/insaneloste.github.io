Users possessing order magic; a formidable and enigmatic branch of mystical arts that bestows upon its practitioners the power to not only wield potent spells but to essentially act as architects of reality itself. Those who harness the intricate intricacies of Order Magic possess the profound ability to influence and restructure the very fabric of existence, weaving a tapestry of cosmic harmony and balance. This mystical discipline stands in stark contrast to its counterpart, Chaos Magic, which seeks to disrupt and destabilize the natural order of things.
