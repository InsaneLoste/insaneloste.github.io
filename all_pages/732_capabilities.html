Users of this ability are either a member of the Aigamuxa or can mimic the traits of such, the Aigamuxa are horrific monsters from African folklore that haunt deserts and other remote areas, seeking out humans and animals to devour - they are distinguished by having their eyes on the soles of their feet, thus must stand on their hands in order to see prey, despite this they are terrible opponents with immense strength and speed as well as extremely long and deadly teeth.
