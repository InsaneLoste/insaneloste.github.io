User can create entire environments, either from nothing or by shaping existing environments to the form they want, where they want it. The Environment can become part of them and whatever happens with the environment is decided by them. They can create environments ranging from purely physical areas that occupy normal space, to mythical, conceptual, metaphysical, extra-dimensional, abstract or even simulated and immaterial ones, as well as anything that exists within them, from objects such as structures to living or non-living entities.
