The user is capable of getting a hold of the place (or plane of existence) where all forms of concepts and ideas reside and rest, if not originate from. In contrast to Concept Manipulation, this controls concepts to a more marked degree of connection to the point of not only manipulating the concepts' contents themselves but their totality of it, for they command both the "elements" and "place". One might argue the user is regarded as sovereign of the plane to an extent.
