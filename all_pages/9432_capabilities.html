The user with this power is able to have their senses intact despite lacking any facial features, whether that would be their eyes, ears, etc. Having no facial properties is normally perceived as a defect, but in this case, as this form of facelessness is definitely a power in terms of origin due to not only giving the subject the immunity to destructive sources and having their senses gone, but despite having possibly their original senses lost once obtained, a new kind of unique senses will then begin to manifest that allows them to perceive, smell, hear, etc, but not in the same way other beings do.
