The user can create, shape and manipulate hellish infectious essence/substances, capable of necrotizing, paralyzing and/or infecting anyone or anything in is path. In addition, demonic disease is capable of inflicting excruciating pain, both physically and spiritually tormenting the victim. It has the potential to corrupt one's soul, and is very effective against the holiest of supernaturals, such as angels. More powerful users can even ultimately summon/resurrect/create evil spirits, nosokinetic entities and/or demons from Hell.
