The user can use propulsion to maneuver and move around easily and quickly, whether it be jet propulsion, helicopter propulsion or other kinds of propulsion such as just their muscles. The user can perform gymnastics and acrobatics well with this ability, and may use this ability while flying, or on the ground, in water, or any combination of the prementioned modes of transport. The user may create propulsion from any point of their body, or a fixed point in their body.
