The user with this ability either is or can transform into an Hecatoncheir (mult. Hecatoncheires, Hundred-Handed Ones, also Centimanes (Latin: Centimani) or Hundred-Handers), a primal giant with a hundred arms and fifty heads. They have incredible strength and ferocity that surpassed that of all Titans whom they helped overthrow by throwing rocks as big as mountains, one hundred at a time.
