The user can take on the abilities of a Hitotsume Nyudo or are one, a Hitotsume Nyudo is an extremely evil demonic giant from Japanese folklore that appears as a massive humanoid with one eye - they often parade around with many other yokai and human slaves, attracting attention from travelers nearby, however any who stop to witness the parade risk being attacked by the cruel and violent spirit, who takes great pride in the many creative and destructive ways they can kill, capture or devour those that get in their way.
