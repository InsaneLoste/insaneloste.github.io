User with this ability either is or can transform into a yeti, a folkloric ape-like creature taller than an average human, that is said to inhabit the Himalayan mountains. According to 21st century sightings, yetis are said to stand from 8 to 10.5 feet tall, have a furry coat of brown, white, reddish or black hair and to resemble a huge, upright walking ape.
