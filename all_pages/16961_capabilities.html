A user with this ability either is or can transform into a Monarch Deity, a god/goddess who represents or is associated with the nobility, the peerage, kingship, queenship, control, power, politics, nobility, influence, leadership, and sovereignty. They are usually revered by those in power, those in a society's aristocracy, and those that seek power. These deities were often worshiped as God-Kings or Goddess-Queens. Monarch Deities were somewhat common in mythology and were important among ancient and current civilizations. More often than not, this type of deity was usually the chief deity of its respective pantheon.
