User with this ability either is or can transform into a Hone-onna (bone woman), a female yokai who, depending on the version, either kills men by extracting their lifeforce or by grabbing their hands and holding them until the victim becomes a skeleton himself, or manifests herself as a skeletal woman. They can appear as human and usually appear as beautiful, but very skinny lady carrying a red lantern in shape of a peony flower and visit men in attempt to sleep with them.
