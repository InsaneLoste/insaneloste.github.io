User can create, shape and manipulate smoke of an ancient primordial nature. As opposed to regular Smoke Manipulation, users of this power are deities, avatars, or living embodiment of smoke. In many cases users of this power will be able to override other forms of Smoke Manipulation as smoke is the domain of the user. Similar to other primordial elements, the raw potency and primeval nature of this power enables the user to easily overpower immunities & resistances to smoke-based abilities.
