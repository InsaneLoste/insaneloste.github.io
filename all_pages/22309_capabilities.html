User with this ability either is or can transform into an shaitan (plural: shayatin. Feminine: Shaitana(h) and Shaytana(h), literally: unruly person, devil, deuce, satan, accuser, adversary, astray or distant), an evil type of jinn, comparable to demons, in Islamic theology and mythology. Usually, shaitans are the worst of the jinn being regarded as the offspring of Iblis, but other beings, such as tyrants and fallen angels are also referred to as shaitans.
