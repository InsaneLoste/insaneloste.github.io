• Does not work on mindless beings (corpses, animated objects, robots, etc.).
  • May be limited to a certain range to work, including touch only.
  • May be limited to one target at a time.
  • May be unable to "turn off" their power and constantly have to hear thoughts.
    • Since the user constantly hears thoughts, this limitation makes mind reading ineffective in large crowds
    • Hearing multiple people's thoughts could cause the user to go insane from their mind trying to process everything.
  • May be unable to read amnesiacs (people whose lose their memories)
  • May not be able to read impulsive actions (done without a plan)
  • Users of Psychic Shield (highly resistant), Psychic Immunity (impervious) and Indomitable Will (too stubborn)
  • Users of Thought Regulation.
  • May get overwhelmed by too many thoughts, especially the ones which are too complicated (e.g. a genius' mind which is doing calculus and scientific formulas), detailed (e.g. an artist or photographer's mind which have all kind of photographic memories), insane/morally corrupted (e.g. "death is salvation" , "burning folks to death is helpful" , "I will create my idea of the perfect world by genocide", "everybody must perished for god's sake" ), and/or ones which don't make any sense (e.g. "2+2=5", "a one-ended line grand plan" , "two or three circle equal turn around" , "use only one square circle for this" ). Persons possessing Alien Mind might be too dangerous to read.
  • Depending upon the user's ability, skill, and tolerance, attempting to read certain minds (e.g., genius , too detailed, mentally insane, and/or those who don't make the proper sense) may be challenging, impossible, or self-destructive.
  • Unless one is multilingual or has the automatic ability to translate and decrypt, ability may not work if the target is thinking in alternate languages (e.g., Ancient Sumerian , Ancient Egyptian, Ancient Greek, Old Persian), or encrypted codes (e.g., Morse Code)
  • The more intense the thought, the louder it sounds to the user
