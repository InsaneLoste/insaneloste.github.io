• Could be constantly active.
  • Immunities of this level can often be overcome by sufficiently superior versions of the powers they normally guard against.
    • Conceptual Fire Manipulation overcomes Fire Immunity.
    • Higher-Spatial Manipulation overcomes Spatial Immunity.
  • Creative opponents may be able to "work around" the immunity.
    • A user of Fire Immunity probably isn't immune to being hacked or smashed to death by masses of solidified fire, or more immediately, from being suffocated by having fire consume their air supply.
    • A user of Geokinetic Immunity could likely still suffocate by being buried in earth or be launched into the sky to fatally crash down.
    • Telekinetic Immunity is of limited value against any opponent with the intelligence &/or capacity to pick up and weaponize another substance with their telekinesis, such as using sand or air to secure a grip on their target if not worse.
  • May only be effective to the powers and abilities of others.
    • If the user possesses other self-altering abilities, such as Telekinesis or Molecular Manipulation, they might still be affected by their own abilities.
    • If the user possesses other self-supporting powers, such as Regenerative Healing Factor or Affinity they may still be active.
  • Immunity doesn't necessarily mean one can completely ignore what they're immune to. E.g. a Geokinetic Immunity user could probably still be knocked around by having masses of stone hurled into them, or maybe even be restrained by it even if it can't directly hurt them.
  • Might be nonselective and affect even beneficial powers.
    • May be immune to all powers, including Healing abilities.
    • If this power is kept even after death, then Resurrection would be impossible.
  • If the user possesses any other powers, they may be canceled out or rendered unusable.
  • Indirect attacks can still affect the user.
  • Immunity Bypassing
  • Useless against Meta Immunity Bypassing
