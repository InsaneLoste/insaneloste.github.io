Users of this ability either is or can gain the traits of undead giants, giants who continue to exist even after death either as gigantic corporeal beasts or in some cases as evil ghosts. Already formidable creatures in life, the undead giants simply grow even stronger when they are turned undead, often being used either as engines of mass-destruction by necromancers or undead armies but also sometimes being the commanders of their own legions of undead. Some, such as the infamous Jack-In-Irons, are solitary monsters and all of them are greatly feared as some of the more powerful subtypes of the undead.
