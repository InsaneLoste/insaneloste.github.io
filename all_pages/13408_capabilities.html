Users of this ability can either mimic the traits of a jumen or are one, jumen are a type of ugly humanoid yokai closely linked to the nikurashi. Like them, the jumen have very little history in terms of lore outside being depicted in certain art, where they are always extremely ugly old men who are believed to be a mockery or parody of the word "grimace", of which their hideous faces resemble, despite their horrendous appearance they are largely harmless yokai.
