A user with this ability either is or can transform into the Lagarfljót Worm. The Lagarfljót Worm is a lake monster purported to live in Lagarfljót, a lake by the town of Egilsstaðir. The first sighting is generally conceded to be the 1345 "marvel" seen at Lagarfljót century chronicle, and recorded in the medieval annals of the 15th century. The serpent at the lake has been mentioned in 16th century world maps, a 17th century chronicle, and a baroque poem from the 17th century chronicle.
