• The ability only allows one to predict attacks however does nothing to offset the difference in physical capabilities between combatants. The ability is ineffective against opponents who are either too fast or strong to adequately defend against or avoid. Put simply, the ability is of little use if the user cannot properly defend or avoid the attacker in spite of knowing their next move.
  • May not be able to dodge multiple attacks coming from every direction.
  • May not be able to dodge Homing Effect attacks or attacks that cannot be dodged.
  • Users are unable to predict movements from opponents with Unpredictability.
  • May be dependent on the user's state of mind and concentration.
  • Doesn't directly provide defense from the attack.
  • May not be able to cope if the predicted attack is perfectly attuned with the actual assault or if the user reacts slower and unable to do so in time.
  • Absolutely defenseless against an Absolute Attack.
