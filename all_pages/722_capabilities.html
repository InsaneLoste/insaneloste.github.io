The user is immune to the negative effects of aging. Because of this, the user will always be at their physical prime. With this power, one could play sports well, regardless of age. Also with this power, one could have an overall strong body, no matter how old they are. Their condition does not deteriorate with age at all, and the user remains at optimal condition and can-do things that may require being younger to be able to be done in the first place. The user will also be immune to aging abilities, such as Age Acceleration or Reversal.
