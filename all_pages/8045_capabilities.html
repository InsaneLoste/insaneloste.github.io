User with this ability either is or can transform into an Emere, a child who can travel between the spiritual and physical world at will, believed to be extremely pretty, and have seductive powers. They are spirits in disguise, misrepresenting death as life, and are believed to be more powerful than witches. Emere are impatient beings and want the best of Heaven and Earth, they most often die on a particular day of joy. On wedding days, when having their first baby, graduation from university etc. depending on the degree of happiness the event might cause.
