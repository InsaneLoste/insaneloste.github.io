The user can impose absolute and irresistible commands upon anything, including themselves, as well as natural and supernatural or living and non-living existences. The user can grant anyone anything or themselves any power simply by commanding it so. Though commands are usually spoken in words, the user's intentions are what truly matters; they can impose complex commands with a single word, through a specific gesture or a sheer act of will, unquestioningly bending anything and everything to their will with all their intents and meaning behind it.
