The user can form various kinds of armor around their body or shape it from various substances/forces for protection and physical augmentation. Some may possess enough control over their exoskeleton to shape it into new forms and create various objects, such as tools, weapons, and modes of transportation from its mass. Typically, this exoskeletal form can either manifest as an energy field centered on the user, function like a suit or even a second body of varying size, shape and overall detail surrounding the user's own or even as a separate entity that serves as a extension of the user as well as the user's guardian or servant.
