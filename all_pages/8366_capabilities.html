The user is an energy vampire; an entity that feeds on the energy of other beings in order to live. They can use their energies to fuel their strength, speed, senses, endurance, and agility, extend their life span. Due to this, users need to feed constantly, like a vampire needs to eat blood constantly. Users are traditional vampires, but they possess a unique ability to use absorbed energies to acquire energetic abilities and even transform into new pure force.
