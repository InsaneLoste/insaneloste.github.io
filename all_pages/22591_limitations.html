• Singularity
  • Limited to skills & talents, cannot copy powers.
  • Accumulation may cause a mental overload.
  • May have a time limit for how long the skills are retained.
  • May only be able to copy a limited number of skills at a time.
  • May need to be within a certain distance.
  • May need physical contact.
  • May not be able to access full capabilities of skills.
  • May be able to copy skills only in inferior/weakened form.
  • May lose replicated skills and need to re-replicate again to retain them.
  • Some users may need help choosing what skills they get.
