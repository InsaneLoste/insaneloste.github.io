The user can channel their energy into a high-density state. This said force takes the form of fire which they can manipulate in a variety of ways. This is simply the user's energy taking on the properties of combustion. As such, they can use that same energy to alter the properties of fire however they choose and manipulate personal volatile forces. This allows their flames to do what earth flames don't. They can also channel this potent force to control other sources of energy and fire in general.
