Users of this power can take on the powers of the Rephaite or are a member of this species. The Rephaite were a tribe of giants found in the Bible who stood much larger than an average human and often quarreled with the early Hebrew people; the most infamous of all the Rephaite would be Og, a mythological king who was said to be so large, his bed was over 9 cubits in length (roughly equal to 14 ft (4 meters) in today's standards).
