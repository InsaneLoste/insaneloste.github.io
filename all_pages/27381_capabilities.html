Users possess the traits, attributes and/or abilities to transform into, or be/is a Weretiger, a human or humanoid being with the ability to transform into a Tiger or an anthropomorphic tiger-like creature. In myths and legends, most notable European, African, Asian, and Indian, this transformation is often associated magical powers that the person has been endowed with, in other cases, this can be seen as a curse and/or lycanthropic affliction via a bite or scratch from an actual Weretiger.
