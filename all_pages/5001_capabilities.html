The user can transfer the mind/spirit/consciousness/soul of oneself and/or others into another body or vessel. which grants them a variation of immortality. When done on oneself, depending on the user and or other people they can even retain all of their powers/abilities and skills, however this may not always be the case depending on their abilities and or the Consciousness. Depending on the user, they may have lifeless, empty bodies for their mind to possess, possessing a comatose/brain-dead person, or just possessing someone else.
