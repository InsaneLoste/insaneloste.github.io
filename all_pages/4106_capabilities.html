The user can create explosives and other volatile substances from their base chemical ingredients. Users of this power may create crude fertilizer-based explosives or manufactured types of explosives such as RDX or ANFO. They can tailor the explosive to achieve various desired effects on the target, creating explosives that generate higher peak pressures or longer enduring shock waves. Other types of explosives that can be created by users of this power include pyrotechnics, thermobaric, fuel-air mixtures, and binary explosives.
