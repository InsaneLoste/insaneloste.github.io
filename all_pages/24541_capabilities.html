Symbiosis (from Greek συμβίωσις, symbíōsis, "living together", from σύν, sýn, "together", and βίωσις, bíōsis, "living") is a powerful and interactive relationship between users and other beings/entities has when becoming hosts to another being/entity that lives in or on the user that naturally advantages them together, generally granting the host gains enhanced condition and new powers/abilities while the symbiote by nature requires the essence of the host in order to survive.
