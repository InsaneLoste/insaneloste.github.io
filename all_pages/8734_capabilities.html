Users can conjure any form of entities of any to all kinds into reality. Entities that are conjured are not necessarily just summoned into existence but can be created or even manifested into being as either duplicates or the original, all having a powerful and mystic connections to their conjurer who potentially has dominance and control over the entities to do the bidding of their will who can either can temporarily or permanently redefined their status empowering them with new abilities, powers, biology, etc all by the command of their conjurer.
