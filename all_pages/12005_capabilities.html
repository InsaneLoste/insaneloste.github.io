The user can create or summon large forces to overtake an entire area and to overwhelm an enemy with vast numbers. The user may create such a force through Replication of one or a few individuals, or through Self-Replication. The large force may or may not be connected through a Hive Mind. The strength of the resulting horde is associated with the very large quantity of summoned/created/replicated fighters and is not necessarily reflective of any coordinated actions or tactics. Individual members of the hoard/swarm are usually treated as expendable.
