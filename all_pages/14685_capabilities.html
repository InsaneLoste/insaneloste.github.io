The user can perform a variety of magical acts by using a Dagger, which can bestow/endow with its own magic providing its user with magics they cannot access alone, or the user can use it to channel their inner magics/powers into it and even acting as an augmentation tool. On certain occasions, they can also be able access magic without a Dagger, using the object as special assistance like an aid, even adding power they can take from others to their own by stabbing others or other forms of energy direction. These types of Daggers can even kill supernatural beings that can't normally be killed in certain circumstances.
