A user with this ability either is or can transform into a wrath deity, a god/goddess who represents or is associated with wrath. This grants them absolute knowledge and power over them, their concepts and principles. Wrath deities are often worshiped by those, be it individually or as a group, who possessed considerable rage and wrath. Others who are less wrathful might worship this deity to merely placate them. Wrath deities weren't a common feature of worship in polytheistic religions but were important among ancient and current civilizations.
