The user either is or can transform into an Alraune, a monstrous plant described by alchemists as taking a form resembling that of a human female with the traits of a plant, most commonly a flower or vice-versa. An Alraune is said to be born when the blood of executed men makes contact with fertile soil and the seeds of plants use them as nourishment to grow instead of water, eventually sprouting into a human/plant hybrid.
