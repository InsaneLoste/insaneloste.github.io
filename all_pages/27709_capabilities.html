Users of this ability can either take on the traits of a Yateveo or are one, a Yateveo is a classic example of folklore hoax (sometimes known as "fakelore") and was based on tales of man-eating plants from across the world - though the specific Yateveo myth is often associated with Africa, like most "fakelore" creatures the Yateveo was a modern invention and weaved into far older folklore to give it a greater sense of mystery and weight.
