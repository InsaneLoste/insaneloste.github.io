Users are beings with superpowers, special or extraordinary superhuman abilities that are greater than what is considered normal, granting them a superior status in mind and/or body that make them greater than that of the average member of their species. In many cases, the user is also able to gain tremendous powers of highly advanced levels. This includes, but is not limited to, superhumans, aliens, angels, demons, gods, vampires, werewolves, hybrids, etc. Most users of this power are still mortal but some may be immortal.
