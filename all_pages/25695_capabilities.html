The user either is or can transform into a Demigod of transcended levels. Demigods are normally of lesser beings compared to actual Deities being way above them, which is a common archetype in all Half-Gods. However, Transcendent Demigods are the direct opposite of this notion. Unlike most Demigods, the user would be a Demigod with a scale advanced enough to compare with the actual deities themselves, being as powerful or even surpassing some of the gods.
