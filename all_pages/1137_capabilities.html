A user with this ability either is or can transform into an anarchy deity, a deity associated with anarchy, lawlessness, sedition, insurrection, rebellion, uprisings, dissidence, defiance and all their aspects. This grants them absolute knowledge and power over them, their concepts and principles.  They are often worshiped by anarchists, dissidents, rebels, insurrectionists, terrorists, and freedom fighters. Anarchy deities aren't a common feature of worship in polytheistic religions but were important among ancient and current civilizations.
