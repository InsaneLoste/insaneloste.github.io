Users, either innately or through training, are masters of wielding a tessen, weaponized Japanese hand fans designed for use in warfare. They are known as a whole as Japanese war fans and are split into several variants, like the gunsen and gunbai, with the main type being the tessen, thus users' proficiency might also extend to them. Unlike normal handheld fans, Japanese war fans' sole purpose is for combat, and are usually made of harder materials, like metal.
