The user can move and attack normally, even when their body has no flesh, for example not having muscles or skin to keep oneself upright, or having one's flesh disabled or useless. The user can still move and attack, even if affected by something that would normally take away their ability to not fall down as a skeleton into a pile of bones. The user can walk, run, jump, punch, roll, kick, swing, etc. like they had flesh (or something similar for their structure), even though they have none, or not at the moment, anyway.
