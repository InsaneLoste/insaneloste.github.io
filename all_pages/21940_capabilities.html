A user with this ability either is or can transform into a Sea Nymph. They are nymphs and minor goddesses associated with and presiding the seas and other bodies of salt waters. There are five different types of Sea Nymphs in mythos, the Limnads, the Haliae, the Nereids, the Oceanids and the Tritonides. All possessing the same powers with slightly different functions and born from different deities assuming their fathers names as the names of their species.
