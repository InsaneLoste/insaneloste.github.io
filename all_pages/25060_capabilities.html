The user can create or wield weaponry with power over warp energy, which grants the user a wide variety of teleportation based effects. They can strike at an opponent and warp away pieces of them at a time, fabricate indestructible ridged arms made out of spatial or dimensional boundary's or simply warping away a target to an undisclosed location on earth or another reality altogether. This ability is not only specific to mystic weapons but also technologically advanced weapons. The power of the weapons depends on what they were created for or what level of antimatter-based ability they possess.
