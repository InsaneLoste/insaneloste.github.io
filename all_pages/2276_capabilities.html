Users of this ability can either mimic the traits of a Azuki Baba or are one, a Azuki Baba is a type of man-eating yokai-hag from Japanese folklore that takes the form of a hideous old hag dressed all in white, singing in a husky, ugly voice, appearing only during the hours of twilight on rainy or misty nights they practice a similar song to the Azuki Arai but they do not do so for pranks as they will follow through on the morbid threat to catch and devour humans.
