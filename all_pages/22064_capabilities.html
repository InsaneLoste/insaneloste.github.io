The user can instantly and perfectly replicate themselves, often numerous times. These self-duplicates are sometimes created directly from the user, often by their user splitting into multiple individual beings while also being able to recombine their clones at will. Most users have both of these abilities (if subconsciously), copying their clothes/equipment along with their body and every ability or other attribute the user has or want their replicas to have. Usually, the original user will be able to maintain control over all copies.
