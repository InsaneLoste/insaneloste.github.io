A user of this ability is, either innately or through training, a master of how to throw parties of any kind, whether it be an event like a wedding, a holiday or just a party for fun. They can know what foods to make, what place the party can be organized, what entertainers (e.g.: clowns, DJs, magicians etc.) needs to be on the party, what music to play, what games to make for the party, know how to decorate or otherwise know how to make the best party ever.
