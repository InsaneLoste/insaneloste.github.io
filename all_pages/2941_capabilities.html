The user either is or can transform into a black dog, (also called Barghest, Black Shuck, Cù Sìth, Moddey Dhoo (or Mauthe Dhoo), Gytrash (or Guytrash), Oude Rode Ogen, Tibicena, Dip, Hairy Jack, Padfoot, Skriker, Churchyard Beast, Shug Monkey, Capelthwaite, Hateful Thing, Swooning Shadow, Bogey Beast. Although the Church Grim is not a Barghest or Shuck, it can also take the form of a large black dog, a being found primarily in the folklores of the British Isles. The black dog is essentially a nocturnal apparition, often associated with the Devil or a Hellhound. It is generally supposed to be larger than a normal dog, and often has large, glowing eyes, often associated with electrical storms, crossroads, places of execution and ancient pathways.
