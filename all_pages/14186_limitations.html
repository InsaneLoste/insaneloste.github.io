• Light Negation/Photokinetic Immunity
  • May rely upon being out in light for any kind of strength at all, or at least for its supernatural abilities, and may lose said abilities when out of direct light sources for a set amount of time.
  • Darkness Weaponry can fairly counter this.
  • May not be able to overpower Transcendent Weaponry.
  • Weapon may require a source of power such as an item.
