The user can gain powers by hosting or bonding with others of one's bloodline, be they genetically related individuals, clones or those with spliced DNA. The entity can have various origins (divine, demonic, spiritual, etc.). It can even be a being that is made up of bloodline energy or power. Whatever the source of its being, by linking to the entity, the host is able to wield potent power related to one's genetics. As with most forms of Symbiosis, it can come with a price.
