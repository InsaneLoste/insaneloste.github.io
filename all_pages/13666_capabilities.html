Users of this ability can either mimic the traits of a Korori or are one, a Korori is a disease-spirit of ancient Japanese folklore that was given the task of spreading cholera and in many ways also embodied the dreaded disease, which caused many deaths in Japan - the Korori was envisioned as a demonic yokai that would visit areas striken with Cholera and spread it further, they were also seen as ghouls that would feed on the corpses of those that had succumbed to the fatal plague.
