Users can manipulate souls with draconic properties, essentially, souls that comes from a dragon. the user can also harness dragon souls to use spiritual energy/forces. Some users obtain this power from absorbing the souls from dragons, learning from spiritual dragons or even coming from a long line of soul dragons. Because of its unique properties, the user can do many things that normal souls cannot do, such as possessing near limitless spiritual energy, gaining unique power and/or abilities or even acquire dragon-like traits.
