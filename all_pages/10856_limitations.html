• Lower ghosts are unable to possess this ability.
  • Users are unable to affect other types of beings with this ability outside ghosts themselves.
  • Users of this great power may have absolute control over others of their own kind, however, they can be temporally overthrown by several major ghosts at once if caught off guard. This is only temporary as these ghosts or beings of this power are naturally more powerful.
  • Some users of this power can not be killed because it could take any created ghosts by them with them to the back to the grave, effectively killing them as well.
