• Divine avatars may or may not be necessarily immortal.
  • If they have a fixed purpose, after that purpose is fulfilled, they may return to the original being.
  • May be bound by the laws of fate and time, and as such also have to experience joys and sorrows equally.
  • Are inferior to their truly divine counterparts in some ways.
  • It may be troublesome for the original being to resume their original form after they assume a partial avatar sometimes.
  • Some may not be Supreme Beings.
