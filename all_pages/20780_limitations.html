• Sound Absorption/Sound Negation
    • Completely negated by Inaudibility.
  • Blocked by powers that can prevent/dampen soundwaves (Air Manipulation, Force-Field Generation, etc.).
  • Users may have sensitive ears, which can be painful because other sounds may throw off this ability's effectiveness or it can be overloaded if there are too many sensory impressions.
  • Depends on how user's powers work, certain things may not be detectable.
  • Users with Supernatural Speed or Absolute Speed may be too fast to detect.
