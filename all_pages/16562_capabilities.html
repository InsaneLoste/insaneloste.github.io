Users of this ability can either mimic the traits of a Nando Baba or are one, Nando Baba are a type of hag yokai that resides in sheds, cupboards or store-rooms, especially ones that are very dirty and dark - they are not hostile to humans and just wish to be left alone, if startled they may attack humans by screaming and chasing them but will quickly be scared away before they do any true harm.
