The user either is or can transform into a mummy of a godly scale. Usually associated with the dead or the underworld they tend to possess abilities reflecting that. They have untold power and have almost no weaknesses, as a standard mummy does. A transcendent Mummy have unfathomable powers rivaling that of gods, for in their time in the afterlife they acquired even greater dynamic prowess and prestige than when they were alive. Transcendent mummies are usually created from the deification of mummified pharaohs and other high-ranking officials, so they gain an accentuated and godly form of lordship over their domain.
