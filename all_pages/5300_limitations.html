• Certain Overburst Powers/Special Attacks can't be reversed.
  • Absolute Attacks are unable to be countered.
  • May take damage when countering an attack and as such can be struck down if they do not have the strength to counter that attack.
  • There's always the chance of misperceiving an attack.
