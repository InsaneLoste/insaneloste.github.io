The user can utilize the Life and Death Arts, a specialized system of techniques based around combined vital and necrotic forces. Life and Death are two of the most fundamentally conceptual dual aspects of existence. In the presence of vital essence, there must also be necrotic essence. These forces form the bases of life forms and their lifespans, existential circumstances and even the possibility of phenomena such as reincarnation, resurrection and the acquisition and/or conferment of immortality. By engaging in esoteric training, one can learn to wield the blended powers of life-force and death-force in a variety of unique ways.
