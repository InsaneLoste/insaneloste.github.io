Users are able to wield a spears or spear-like weaponry with supernatural proficiency in speed, power, dexterity, and skill, allowing them to perform seemingly impossible feats with ease, such as piercing through nigh invulnerable or intangible materials, create pockets of vacuum with simple thrusts, cleave through entire landmass such as mountains while creating fissures, rend large objects into mere pieces with ease, block or deflect things that should be impossible to perceive, pierce targets from a short distance without moving, and manipulate the length of the spear-shaped.
