The user has undergone mutation that caused heightened abilities and mutated physiology through data that may cause the user to change drastically either physically, mentally or spiritually. The user may have received various powers over time depending on what kind of power changed them and the nature of data manipulation. While digital mutation is a mutation of the digital form, the mutation may manifest in a character's physical world form or physiology if the character relies on their digital form as a template for building, maintaining, modifying, or rebuilding their physical world presence. Users of Advanced Technology Self-Restoration would be highly susceptible to digital mutation impacting their physical form.
