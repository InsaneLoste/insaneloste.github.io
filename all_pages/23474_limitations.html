• May need to perform certain actions/fulfill conditions to use magic, ranging from specific words/songs, inscription/incantation, certain hand/body moves, materials that may be consumed or even extensive rituals.
  • Incorrect pronunciation and word choice of a spell may have negative effects (such as the spell being thrown back at the user, producing a different effect than expected or part of reality may fall apart).
  • May need to use special artifacts such as spell books, herbs, and talismans.
  • May be limited on how many spells they can cast.
  • May require concentration to work.
  • Certain spells may only work for as long as the caster is alive.
  • Certain spells may be limited to a specific group of spell casters.
  • Certain spells may have a negative effect on nature and require a balance to the universe. For instance, if one person is brought back to life, another will die in the process.
  • All Spells Require Arcane Power to fuel their magic, such as Ambient Mana (Ley Lines, Magical Field, Otherworldly Power), power from other mystical beings (Gods, Angels, Demons, Dragons, Eldritch Abominations) or other sources of mystical energy.
    • More powerful spells may require more magical power and training to work.
      • Untrained novices can easily misuse a spell creating unpredictable results.
  • Some spells may taint the user's soul and turn them down the path of evil/madness/disorder, and therefore are best left unknown.
  • Some spells may simply need to be spoken in reverse to be negated.
  • Once a spell is casted, it may not be able to be altered.
  • Overuse may lead to physical and/or mental damage, temporary/permanent loss of magic or death.
  • Useless against people with Spell Negation and Spell Immunity.
