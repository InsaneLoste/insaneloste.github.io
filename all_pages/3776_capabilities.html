The user can utilize the Cards Arts, a specialized system of techniques based around card-based power. There are various types of powerful decks that can grant their users supernatural abilities; however, properly wielding them to bring about desirable effects can still take considerable training and study. In some cases, an individual may actually have a card-based physiology that allows them power associated with particular decks. By undergoing specialized training, one can learn to wield this card power to exhibit skills and techniques ranging from enhanced physical ability to energy manipulation to healing.
