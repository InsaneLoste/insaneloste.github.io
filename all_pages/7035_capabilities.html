The user has a draconic form of divinity and divine powers that them an immense amount of raw power beyond the mundane, making the draconic users divine being by nature, a status which can be obtained several different ways. Users can gain this type of divinity by having draconic ancestry, being transformed into a divine dragon either by another more powerful divine dragon, a deity, higher being, or by faith, channel, act as a vessel for, have the power/soul of an dragon, mimic/steal powers from an dragon, or be enhanced/evolved to the point of godhood. However, at times users of draconic divinities are more powerful or even weaker than other deities depending on the type of divine being they are in question.
