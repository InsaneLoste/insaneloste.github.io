The user can utilize the Anti-Magic Arts, a specialized system of techniques based around anti-magic power. This training curriculum entails skills devoted to the absorption, dilution, destruction and/or outright negation of magical energies and phenomena (magic, mana, spells, etc.). The skill set may actually entail the usage of Magical forces; however, the end result is the opposition of magic itself. Through specific practices, one can learn to use the power of anti-magic to enable feats ranging from physical augmentation to energy/matter manipulation.
