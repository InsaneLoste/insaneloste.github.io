The user is able to become or is a deity in Manichaean mythology. Manchaeism was a religion that originated in Aramaic regions and was inspired by Mesopotamian and Gnostic mythology, but was primarily worshipped in China. Manichaeism is a dualistic mythology, meaning a mythology with only two deities. Both deities have similar cosmic powers and equal in strength but are different in that Manichaeism focuses on a good deity of light fighting against a bad deity of darkness.
