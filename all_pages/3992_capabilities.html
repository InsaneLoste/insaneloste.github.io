A user with this ability either is or can transform into a Chalkydri. The Chalkydri are dragon-like heavenly beings mentioned in Christian mythology that are said to dwell near the Sun alongside other flying elements referred to as phoenixes. The appearance of the Chalkydri is said to be mesmerizing with the colors of the rainbow running through them, bearing the head of a crocodile and the feet and tail like that of a lion, each having up to twelve wings. At dawn all the chalkydri break into song with their counterparts, alerting the birds of the world for a new day to rejoice.
