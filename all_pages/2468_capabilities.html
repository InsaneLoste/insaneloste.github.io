The user has absolute power and control over all beasts and animals, they have unlimited control and power over all manner of creatures ranging from the mundane (dogs, cats, turtles) to the most powerful and mythical (dragons, sphinxes, phoenixes). They control them to do their bidding like, help them during situations, use them against foes, use them to see locations and get information about a particular place, use them for flight. Some can see through the creatures' eyes that is controlled, allowing them to spy on others. User can also understand or communicate with them, hence creating and strengthening friendships. the user's power transcends any potential limitations and resistance.
