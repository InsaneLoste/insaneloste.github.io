The user can utilize the Banishment Arts, a specialized system of techniques based around banishing power. Practitioners engage in unique training methods revolving around the ability to expel and/or exorcise certain entities (spirits, demons, angels, gods, animals, objects, etc.). This often involves unique gestures, prayers, materials and/or energies utilized to remove an unwanted force from a location or host. Along with ejecting said entities, the user can potentially contain the being and restore the affected location and/or host to their original state. Through unique training, one can learn to wield banishment and/or exorcism abilities to enable combat enhancement, personal fortification (protection of body, mind and spirit) and even the ability sense the presence of unwanted entities that require removal.
