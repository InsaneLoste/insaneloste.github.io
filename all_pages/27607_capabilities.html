User with this ability either is or can mimic/transform into worms, a collective term descripting many different distantly related animals that typically have a long cylindrical tube-like body and no legs. These include annelids (earthworms), nematodes (roundworms), platyhelminthes (flatworms), marine nemertean worms (bootlace worms), marine Chaetognatha (arrow worms) and priapulid worms.
