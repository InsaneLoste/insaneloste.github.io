The user can utilize the Inner Beast Arts, a specialized system of techniques based around inner bestial power. This set of practices deals with one tapping into the essence of the innate creature/entity that dwells within the user. By properly synchronizing body, mind and spirit, one can channel its supernatural nature to exercise a number of powerful skills. As one is inherently gifted with power via the inner beast, the training enables one to practice abilities such as enhanced physiology, healing and combat potency.
