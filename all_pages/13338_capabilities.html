A user with this ability either is or can transform into a Jann, the ancestor and weakest of genies. Janni are shapeshifters who live in the desert, and take the forms of whirlwinds, serpents and white camels. In Arabic, Jann (جان) is another singular to Jinn along with Jinni (جني), but in modern myths, Jann is a Desert type of Jinn or a Serpent one.
