• Powers must be used on the user in order to copy them, so it will not work if they do not use them on the user.
  • May have to trick others into using their powers on them.
  • Useless if the opponent's power ends up killing them (e.g. Death Inducement or One Hit Kill) unless the user has some power to avoid death (e.g. Immortality or Self-Resurrection).
  • Powers that only affect the user, such as Regenerative Healing Factor, may be impossible to replicate.
  • May only be able to copy the powers for a limited period of time.
