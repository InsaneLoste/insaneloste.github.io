Users of this ability can control, shape and absorb chi that is evil or negative in form. This form of chi is the result of either being corrupted by dark forces or the user being born with it. Regardless, they gain access to powerful dark abilities and forces that grant them incredible power. However, the user's force of will, compassion, and sanity are all lost due to being over taken by evil, eventually leaving them to feel nothing except anger and hatred, and becoming driven by their most aggressive instincts.
