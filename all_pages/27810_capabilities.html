A user with this ability either is or can transform into a Zhenniao, a mythical bird from ancient Southern China. The Zhenniao are depicted as poisonous birds with purple abdomens and green-tipped feathers with long necks and a scarlet beaks. They become poisonous from body to feathers due to eating poisonous viper heads. Their bodies are tainted with deadly poison known as Zhendu or Zhen venom. It is said that dipping the poisonous feather in wine can turn it into deadly poison and just one drop can kill.
