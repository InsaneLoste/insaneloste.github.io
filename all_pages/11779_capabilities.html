The user, either innately or through training, is a master of hematology, the branch of medicine concerned with blood, blood-forming organs, and blood diseases. This enables them to treat diseases that affect the production of blood and its components, such as blood cells, hemoglobin, blood proteins, bone marrow, platelets, blood vessels, spleen, and the mechanism of coagulation, as well as determine DNA evidence related to blood, such as mutations and the like. With this ability, users can prevent blood-related diseases and injuries from happening and/or getting worse.
