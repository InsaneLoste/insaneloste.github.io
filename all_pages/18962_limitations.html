• Users can only violate the laws of physics to a certain extent and cannot rearrange physics on a cosmic scale for their benefit.
  • Most users can only break physics in specific ways, e.g. Raiden can channel electricity through insulated objects.
  • The user cannot directly manipulate physics, they can only manipulate how it affects them.
  • May have a limit to how long they can use this power.
  • Users of Physics Enforcement may affect the user.
