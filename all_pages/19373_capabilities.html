Users can control all kinds of poisonous animals, such as: poisonous snakes, insects, spiders, amphibians, poisonous birds, fish and mammals. Holders of this power can order poisonous animals to do various things, for example, summon them to battle to fight their enemies, or use them to infiltrate places that are usually inaccessible to the user. Naturally, they will also be able to communicate with them, read their thoughts and emotions and form telepathic connections with them. More advanced users can call for/create poisonous animals or even manipulate their genetic composition and DNA to enhance their characteristics and qualities.
