The user can create, shape and manipulate the threads of fate, supernatural strings that embody the lives, experiences and/or destinies of individuals. Also known as the threads of life, in mythology, they are spun into existence, (granting someone life), then they are lengthened/woven (creating a certain amount of time individuals have to live and a particular destiny they fulfill) and if the individual is truly mortal, they are cut (the individual’s life ends), no more different then individuals who are truly immortal, they’re strung would be impossible to cut (the individual’s life endlessly continues).
