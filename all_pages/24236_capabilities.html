The user can utilize supernatural arts, an esoteric class of practices based around a particular paranormal phenomenon (spiritual power, elemental forces, demonic essence, divine essence, mystical arts, psychic power, etc.). By engaging in a specialized training process, one is able to unlock superhuman capabilities that can range from things as simple as enhanced physical condition to large-scale techniques like reality manipulation. While individuals may be born with inherent abilities (such as magic, which is a well-known supernatural art), they must train to unlock their full potential, which can enable a multitude of newfound powers.
