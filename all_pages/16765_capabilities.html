The user can create, shape and manipulate Neodymium, a soft silvery rare earth metal that tarnishes in air, is not found naturally in metallic form or unmixed with other lanthanides, and is usually refined for general use. Although neodymium is classed as a "rare earth", it is a fairly common element, no rarer than cobalt, nickel, and copper, and is widely distributed in the Earth's crust.
