The user can alter the physical fitness of themselves or others. Users may convert Olympic athletes into weak and obese individuals, or transform skinny-armed weaklings into muscle-bound tough-guys. Users can even shift a bulky power lifter into a lean marathon runner (or vice versa), shifting the nature of the target's fitness. This power is not just manipulating fat and muscle; it can enhance or degrade all aspects of physical fitness including cardiovascular fitness, cardiorespiratory fitness, strength, and endurance. Unlike Superpower Manipulation and Power Bestowal, fitness manipulation can only elevate someone to their peak natural potential at most. Thus, a human's fitness could at most be elevated to the level associated with Peak Human Body ... which is still pretty impressive.
