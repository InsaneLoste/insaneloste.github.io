The user either is or can transform into an Elohim, Biblical divine or heavenly spiritual beings belonging to the spiritual realm and were created before the creation of the physical universe. They are generally seen as "lesser gods or minor deities" subservient to The One True Creator Elohim. Primarily, these heavenly beings dwell in The Third Heaven or The Abode of God (God's Heaven) and are assigned with many roles such as carrying out modest tasks as divine messengers of the word of God, As destroyers sent out by God to destroy whatever he wishes, being protectors or divine throne guardians of the boundaries of heaven or sacred spaces, as watchers, guardians and administers over nations assigned to them by The Creator or as heavenly beings who come together in a divine assembly known as "The Divine Council" to help and celebrate The Creator God's decisions.
