• Lava Immunity/Magma Absorption
  • May be unable to create magma, being limited to manipulating only from already existing sources.
  • Control is extremely important. Unconscious use of this power isn't something to laugh at.
  • Distance, mass, precision, etc. depend upon of the knowledge, skill, and strength of the user, and their power's natural limits.
  • Fire Immunity and/or Thermal Resistance isn't always part of the package, so burning yourself is possible.
  • Magma can be slowed/stopped/solidified by water, ice or cold.
  • May not be immune to volcanic gases.
