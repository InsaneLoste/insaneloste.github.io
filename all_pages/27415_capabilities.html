Users, either innately or through training, are masters of wielding whip swords. They come in many forms, mainly a sword with a flexible, whip-like blade such as the urumi, a sword consisting of a series of blade segments strung together on an extendable cord, or a whip with sharp edges/extensions/protrusions. Users can utilize both short and long ranged attacks at various angles and make use of the swords and flexible length to extend the reach of their attack such as turning corners to catch enemies off guards, or even entangle their opponents.
