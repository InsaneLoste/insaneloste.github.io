User with this ability either is or can transform into an Archon, mystical celestial beings that are known in Gnostic theology as the several servants of the "creator god" Demiurge. Archons are also the beings that act as mediums between the human race and their creator which could only be obtained through gnosis. The Archons are a species of inorganic beings that emerged in the solar system prior to the formation of the earth. Depending on the source, they can be either angelic or demonic beings
