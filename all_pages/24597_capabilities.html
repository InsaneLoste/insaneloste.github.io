The user can utilize the Symbolic Arts, a specialized system of techniques based around symbolic power. There are abilities that have a unique significance, existing as representations of certain ideas, beliefs, materials or even idiosyncratic aspects like personality, existence, soul or philosophy. By engaging in esoteric practices, one can learn skills that allow for supernatural manifestations based on said representations. The simplest methods can take the form of unique attacks, defenses or transformations that resemble the symbolic essence. At the highest levels, one can learn to manipulate conceptual aspects or natural laws based on the potency of the power being wielded. Though it may depend on whether this is formed from a personification of outside beliefs or internal ideals, one may be capable of personal transcendence through the training.
