• Users that have Absolute Intelligence can easily outsmart the users without a single problem.
  • While the user is far smarter than those of Enhanced Intelligence, they are still susceptible to mistakes, though the difference here is that the mistakes they would commit are very few and far between.
  • At this level, one may experience intense social isolation if not outright asociality due to being unable relate to others who do not share the same intellectual prowess.
  • May draw damaging amounts of energy/calories/sustenance from the body, causing various physical problems.
