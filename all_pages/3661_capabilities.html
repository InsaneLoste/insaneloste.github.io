The user can enter into or is always calm, serene and relaxed regardless of the situation(s) or what happens to themselves or to others around them. Their body, mind, heart and soul are always perfectly clear and relaxed, and their mental fortitude and endurance is unbreakable/unyielding no matter what is happening. Users of this aren't simply apathetic, as they can still feel and experience emotions; rather, it's just that they will remain calm regardless of the emotion they are experiencing.
