In fiction, mana is basically and most commonly portrayed as the magical aspect of life-force (i.e. magical life energy, or 'magical energy with life-force'). Hence, it can be found on a universal scale, as life-force is the fundamental of existence. Magical entities are able to control mana. They can harness this type of energy from not only their own selves but also the ambiance, since everything that exists possesses vitality, as aforesaid. Rare users can create new mana from nothing.
