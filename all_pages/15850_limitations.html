• Since cells can only divide so many times under normal circumstances, accelerating mitosis is to shorten one's life unless one can remove the limit (i.e. regrow telomeres).
  • If user has cancer, accelerating its mitosis would speed up its progression.
  • Slowing down mitosis to decelerate aging may cause wounds to close at a slower rate.
  • Accelerating mitosis may cause malnutrition as cells require nutrients to divide.
