The user can gain powers by symbiotically hosting or linking to a mythic being. There are many types of mythological beings throughout the legends and cultures of the world, each one possessing its own specialized form and set of abilities. By combining with said entities, hosts are capable of wielding any number of preternatural powers related to their nature (elemental manipulation, enhanced physical power, magic, etc.). As with most forms of Symbiosis, it can come with a price.
