• Not everything is encompassed by dimensionality, so whilst being beyond dimensions makes the user unfathomably powerful to anything within dimensionality, they may still be outclassed by other forces beyond or not limited by dimensions.

    • Almighty Laws, the Grand Design, and other similar forces still bind the user.
