The user has the ability to create and/or wield powerful weapons with attributes far superior to those of mundane weaponry such as greater strength, durability, attack power, firepower, range, rate of fire, ammunition type and capacity, damage potential, etc. They may also have been produced by master craftsmen using either conventional or supernatural means to make them unique arms of great power. Some weapons can be enhanced even further. When in the possession of skilled wielders, the weapons can display capabilities beyond the functions of their design.
