The user has control over their own nuclear chemistry. With this power, they can manipulate self-generated nuclear processes and their properties. This includes personal radioactivity, radiation spectra, nuclear fusion, fission, etc. Unlike Personal Nuclear Power, this ability involves more than just controlling personal nuclear reactions. The user is able to manipulate the relationship their matter has with nuclear power. They can achieve a wide array of abilities such as being able to generate and channel large amounts of energy more safely compared to nuclear reactions. Essentially, the user becomes a walking nuclear chemical power plant.
