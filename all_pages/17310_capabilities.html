Users are an omega human; a prime human who obtains a newly enhanced physiology, newfound abilities and a greater level of power by hosting a superior force/source who are deeply connected to and can harness to their will. In having such power through enigmatic source they grow to become a far more immensely powerful beyond all the members of human species, including their extant variants, making them a unique member of their kind, inheriting a new physiological condition, traits, abilities and appearance that reflect both the nature of their power and their humanity.
