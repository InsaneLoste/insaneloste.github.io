The users are spirit mediums, individuals that can perceive, communicate or otherwise interact with the souls/spirits of others. They can banish these entities from an area or summon souls/spirits that will defend them if they were ever to come to harm. They may even have their own soul or spirit enter the different worlds within the afterlife, while having their bodies within the living world entering in a sort of trance-like state. That allows them to return to their body when they're finished with their affairs within the afterlife.
