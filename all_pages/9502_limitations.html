• Sensory Overload
  • Drugs or other means can either negate or remove these senses unless they are reversible.
  • Overloading may be a frequent occurrence.
  • Magic may cause interference.
  • May be dulled by pain if the user is injured.
  • Cannot work on those with Sensory Deception.
  • May be caught by surprise by a third party if the user is too focused on a specific target, which affects their ability to detect other targets.
