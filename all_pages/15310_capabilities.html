The user is able to apply incredible force to their feet, enabling them to deliver a supernatural stomp. While this term has been used to describe many supernatural stomps, the true megaton stomp is packed with 4.18 petajoules of energy. That megaton stomp delivered to a typical human would result in complete obliteration of the body. The effect of a megaton stomp is comparable to a directed nuclear explosion or a supernatural cannon. Users can kick through battle tank armor, smash down powerful fortress walls, and leave massive craters in the earth. Supernatural damage aside, a user must hold supernatural power to control this attack because, by Newton's third law, delivering this stomp should accelerate a 200 lbs user to over 6,000 km/s, exceeding escape velocity and launching them on a direction permanently away from earth.
