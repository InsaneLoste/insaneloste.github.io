The user can gain powers through a symbiotic relationship with an emotional/emotion-based being. These can include creatures that are made of emotional energy, supernatural beings (spirits, monsters, etc.) associated with particular emotions, manifested emotions, etc. By absorbing power or through being a host, the user will be able to wield powerful abilities that are often a reflection of the sentimental entity's nature (i.e., anger grants strength), but as with most forms of Symbiosis, it can come with a price.
