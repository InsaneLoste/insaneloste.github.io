Users either are or can transform into a Jinmenken (Man-Faced Dog), a strange creature with a body of a dog and a human face. The Jinmenken is a Japanese urban legend that became popular during the Edo era. There have been sightings of the creature throughout the years from people walking at night and encountering one. It is said that if one talk to the Jinmenken, it will turn around, revealing its human face, and tells them to leave them alone in a willowy voice. They have been thought to be bad omens and blamed for accidents and disasters. There have been attempts to film and capture the beasts.
