Users of this ability have the ability to take on the traits of a Coblynau or are one, a Coblynau is a type of gnome (or sometimes a benevolent goblin) found in Welsh folklore, closely related to Knockers but of a helpful nature - they are depicted as ugly little humanoids often dressed in traditional mining gear and they would work tirelessly in mines, they would aid miners in finding hidden gems and treasure via making knocking sounds and are well-liked for their friendly personality.
