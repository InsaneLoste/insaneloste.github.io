Users embody gluttony and may gain the ability to gain power off the gluttony of everyone and including themselves. They may also assume the physical traits that are commonly associated with gluttony, such as obesity. Their personality and mannerisms also reflect their crapulence. Some powers that users may gain from embodying gluttony include the ability to destructively consume anything and everything, even on the conceptual level, or even growing exponentially stronger the more they eat or feel gluttony.
