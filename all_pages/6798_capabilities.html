The user possesses divine body parts that contain divine powers directly related to the nature, function and attributes of those parts, giving users powerful abilities that those parts give them. For example, if users has a divine eye, they would have divine sight. Said body parts, being divine in the first place, can be bound to the original users by nature; however, should the users' body parts be removed and taken by another, said individual can possess the powers of the original owners. They may also gain a connection to the original.
