Users, either innately or through training, are masters of wielding naginata, pole weapons and one of several varieties of traditionally made Japanese blades or more commonly known as a Japanese halberd, with incredible expertise in speed, power, and skill, allowing them to perform feats such as stopping and deflecting bullets, decimating large objects or areas, cutting through a solid material such as steel, and even hold back their strength to deal damage to their foes without killing them. They also have greater versatility and advantage in reach compared to weapons like swords. Users may also be proficient in wielding bisentō.
