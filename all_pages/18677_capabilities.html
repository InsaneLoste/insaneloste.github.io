The user possesses their own personal symbiote, an exclusive symbiotic entity that only one person is capable of controlling and accessing its powers. Symbiosis enables its wielder the capacity to attain supernatural abilities through a bond with a particular entity, usually through a physical bond. Inherently, the power is idiosyncratic. Various types of entities are capable of being accessed by multiple individuals, allowing for many users to wield the same set of abilities. With this power, the user is the only one capable of controlling the powers of a specific bonded entity (usually in a particular circumstance or universe). Their symbiotic partnership, being singular, affords them a singular set of powers.
