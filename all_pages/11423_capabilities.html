User with this ability either is or can become a half-Genie. Being a hybrid, it naturally possesses a certain amount of hybrid vitality depending on how proportional your heritage is. While normal genies possess near-godlike level powers, they typically have rules to restrain them. But hybrid genies can possibly circumvent the rules full-blooded genie have, allowing them to have all the power of their genie parent with no limitations to hold them back making them a real force to be reckoned with.
