The user possesses all forms of science, powers, and potential, ascending themselves beyond superhuman, planetary, and mundane scale, achieving a potent scientific variation of nigh-omnipotence. The user naturally can manipulate matter, energy, forces, and particles on a microscopic and macroscopic scale, from operating on a subatomic scale or galactic size. They can affect the upscale structures of any material that exists in the universe and beyond; however, the user only has the potential to accomplish God-like features, not the information to execute them skillfully.
