The user can utilize the Anti-Spell Arts, a specialized system of techniques based around disenchanted power. Anti-Magic Arts utilize powerful supernatural forces that, while potentially capable of various specialized feats, are largely geared towards the destabilization, destruction and/or negation of magical phenomena. In order to wield such power with finely tuned skill, one uses anti-spells, a highly specialized set of practices entailing expressions, figures and even unique objects designed to undo mystical phenomena. Whatever their origins and/or natures, they allow the user to channel anti-magical power for changing the world around them, usually in the form of undoing magic spells.
