The user can create a mecha, vehicular constructs that commonly take the form of biomorphic, pilotable machines, particularly robots, as well as entities or structures. Typically designed for combat, these vehicles are filled with a varying arsenal or weaponry, support, and defensive capabilities and surprising methods of transportation, to serve their bidding under undying loyalty. If the user is high-level then there is no limit in creating a mecha with exceptional size, powers, and abilities for combat.
