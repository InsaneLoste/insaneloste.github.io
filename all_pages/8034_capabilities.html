The user can utilize the Embodiment Arts, a specialized system of techniques based around personified power. There are instances where certain forces, environments or ideas gain a particular manifested form. This earthly form allows for a greater interaction with ordinary beings while still having access to preternatural power. Through specialized training, one can be capable of harnessing the power of these incarnations. Users may act as conduits for the power of said embodied figure. One could summon such beings to serve the user as combatants, defenders or even supporters capable of feats like healing. One may even be able transform into or merge with a personified being. There are even cases where one who engages in techniques is an embodiment, the physical manifestation of anything from natural essences to entire worlds to concepts. However it is accomplished, users could potentially acquire absolute power over whatever the entity embodies.
