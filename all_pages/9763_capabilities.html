Users can turn anything into fiction, from entities to objects to events to entire realities. The user can turn enemies into fiction, thus rendering them harmless as they exist on a fundamentally less complex plane. In some cases, the fictionalized target also becomes susceptible to alteration from higher level entities who can simply imagine or express them in a different way, therefore altering them similar to how real-world readers of fiction can essentially change worlds and characters by imagining or telling them in a different way.
