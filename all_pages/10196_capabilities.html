The user is or can transform into a dragon that is the embodiment/representation of a particular force or concept. They have complete control over there particular force/concept and manipulate ever aspect of it. These dragons can manipulate both natural and uncommon forces that can grant incredible powers of both natural and supernatural to the users beyond that of themselves. Users can get a great number of abilities bound to the nature of the forces that they use and depending on the variant forces that are apart of the universe. Species like these are a rare, powerful type, capable of feats only heard of with the powers of a particular force/concept with all the ferocious force of a dragon.
