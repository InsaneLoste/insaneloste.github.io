The user is a Seraphim Angel, an extraordinarily powerful upper-level angel who represents and is associated with the holy fires of the heavens, being completely infused with it throughout their entire being. Seraphim possesses two, four, and even six wings on their bodies, one or three faces, and a multitude of eyes on their faces and wings, very similar to cherubim angels in many ways. It is said that whoever lays eyes on a seraph would instantly be incinerated due to the immense brightness of their divine power and holy fires. They are depicted as the highest of the angelic hierarchy, standing next to the supreme creator. With near-almighty power, they adore their Lord's actions, authority, presence, and existence, in addition to commanding most of heaven's armies.
