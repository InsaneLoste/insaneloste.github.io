The user can transform others and/or objects into exact, often hive-minded, duplicates of themselves or others, both physically and in persona. The effect is often permanent and irreversible, but some unique individuals may be able to interrupt and reverse the process before it is completed. Users can also assimilate the knowledge, skills, memories and special abilities of their victims and share them with all replicas, becoming exponentially more powerful with each upgrade. If the user dies, then the next replica can become the original one and so on.
