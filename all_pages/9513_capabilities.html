The user can summon their own familiar, beings that are completely loyal and subservient to the user. Familiars are supernatural entities, normally associated with magic/witchcraft, that fulfills the needs of the owner. They often come into their owner's life through a magical contract. The actual type of familiar summoned varies greatly; it is typically a small animal (such as a witch's cat) but can be anything, including demons, goblins or even human beings.
