Users of this power can take on the powers of a Nachtkrapp or are one, the Nachtkrapp is depicted as a malevolent crow-like monster that would attack children that it came across during the hours of night, it had a wide variety of malicious abilities by which to torment humans such as spreading disease and death, though its most favoured activity was to abduct children and take them to its nest where they would be devoured in an extremely vicious manner, with their limbs being removed and finally having their hearts devoured.
