The user has the light/benevolent/good/pure form of divinity and divine powers granting them an immense amount of raw power beyond the mundane, making the light divinity users divine beings by nature, a status which can be obtained several different ways. Users can gain this type of divinity by having a divine ancestry, being transformed into a light divinity either by another more powerful light divinity, a deity, higher being, by faith, channels, acts as a vessel for, the power/soul of a deity, to mimic/steal powers from a light divinity, or be enhanced/evolved to the point of godhood. However, at times users of the light divinity are weaker or stronger than deities depending on the type of divine being they are in question.
