• Like among other regeneration abilities, this level also does have the same exact weaknesses that the other levels have.
    • Cannot reverse Irreversible Destruction.
    • Anti-Regeneration/Healing Erasure/Healing Factor Nullification
      • Destruction/Absolute Destruction
    • Instant death can kill the user before they can regenerate.
      • Death Inducement/One Hit Kill
    • Disintegration/Dissolution/Incineration
    • Destabilization causes the user's healing factor and to become unstable to the point of getting killed.
    • May not include Pain Suppression.
    • Users of Biological Manipulation can render the user helpless.
    • Radiation can affect the user on a cellular level and destroy the user's healing factor.
  • Despite having their regeneration at its peak, the users of this level cannot regenerate completely-destroyed internal organs, their brain, or their limbs.
  • Users can still be killed by extreme means.
  • Doesn't include Disease Immunity.
