The user is a transcendent slayer, that is, a slayer who exceeds the natural limitations of a natural slayer, who possesses knowledge over all things paranormal and power to destroy all manner of creatures in existence be they mystical, supernatural, extraterrestrial or even other transcendent being. User are extremely powerful slayer capable of resisting to the powers and effects of other beings, giving the user an a greater advantage. Unlike most known Slayers, users not only possesses greater powers, but also very powerful weapon, combat skills, and even immunities to hunt down all kinds of creatures, making said users the ultimate supernatural hunter in their existence.
