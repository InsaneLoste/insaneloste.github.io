• The user may be unable to control their overwhelming telekinetic power, resulting in unintentional misuse of their abilities, such as accidental telekinetic nuclear detonations, destroying countries, or unintentionally pulling the moon towards Earth.
  • May not be able to manipulate the properties (molecules, electrons, neutrons, quarks, etc.) of objects; only control them as a whole.
  • Telekinesis Negation and Telekinesis Immunity can oppose the user.
  • User is susceptible to having blunt force preventing complex telekinetic manipulations (Matter/Energy Manipulation at an atomic level).
  • Users should use their powers carefully, as they may be unable to rebuild what they destroy.
