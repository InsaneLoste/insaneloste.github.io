• Soul-based damage can affect users.
  • Unlike an Unfettered Body, since user is not immortal, they can be killed despite being doubly impregnable.
    • Death Inducement, One Hit Kill, Dissolution can instantly kill user.
    • Depending on mechanism of their power, means such as Double Hit Kill, Atomic Dissociation/Atomic Cancellation or molecular powers may kill user.
  • User may be vulnerable to Invulnerability Bypassing/Invulnerability Negation, Immunity Bypassing.
  • Since mind cannot be interacted in any way with, any form of Psychic Communication is inaccessible.
