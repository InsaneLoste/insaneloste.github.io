The user, either innately or through training, is a master of economics, the branch of knowledge concerned with the production, consumption, and transfer of resources and wealth. Many people misinterpret economics to be the study of money or the stock market; however, it is really study people, their choices, and resources. All resources are finite, while everyone's desire is infinite. As such people are forced to make choices on how to spend their resources. The user seeks out which is the best way fulfill those desires while balancing which resources to spend.
