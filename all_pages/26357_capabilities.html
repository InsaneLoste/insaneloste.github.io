The user can manipulate the forces that allow things to unite literally or metaphorically. Not only the user can just fuse or defuse things, but they can prevent fusion. The user can make things that are assimilated or absorbed by something else not happen or vice versa. This ability also affects communities as well, in a different sense. The force that the user wields can even cause a union of humans or animals to disperse or gather at the user's will. This force can act in mystical and physical ways.
