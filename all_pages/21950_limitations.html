• Depending to the strength of the user, the seals can weaken over time.
  • The creatures sealed inside people can potentially weaken their seal over time.
  • Depending on the creature's strength, it could force the person it's sealed in to break the seal.
  • Low-level users can only seal small things.
  • If the seal-carrying person dies, the being sealed may also do so. It depends of the seal, rules of the current universe, and relative power-levels.
  • The seal-carrying person's state of health/mind may be tied to the strength of the seal, so anything that disturbs them may give the sealed being a chance to break out.
  • Users of Anti Storage cannot be sealed.
