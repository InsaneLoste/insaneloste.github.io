• Speed Cancellation
  • May increase the odds of a user injuring themselves without the proper durability, as the extra force they exert also comes back at them as an equally powerful counterforce due to Newton's Third Law of Motion.
  • Still requires the user to have the means/space to use their speed, and thus may be unable to use if the user is properly restrained.
  • May be harder to prevent collateral damage due to the momentum-based nature of this power.
