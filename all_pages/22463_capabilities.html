User with this ability either is or can transform into a silah (Arabic: سعلا أو سعلاة alternatively spelled Si'la or called Si'lat) a supernatural creature connected to the jinn in Arabian folklore. They are described as talented shapeshifters often aligned with sexuality appearing as a human female who seduce men/women. Females tend to marry them or even give birth to a child from a relationship between humans and jinn. All Sila are associated with magic and witchcraft, classifying them as mage class jinn; similar to witches.
