The user is a protector and keeper of dreams and the dream world. The user doesn't necessarily become a dream being, merely a protector that oversees the dream world and is in charge of keeping it in check; this extends to aiding dreamers when they sleep and protecting them from such things as nightmares and evil oneiric entities. Through their position, the user acquires special abilities that allow them to control all aspects of dreams in order to make sure everything within it is in their proper place.
