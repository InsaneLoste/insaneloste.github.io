The user gains the traits of a toilet deity, a type of mythological being believed to safeguard latrines, toilets and sewers. While such deities were ridiculed by outsiders they played vital roles in many cultures across history, due to the fact such places were often hazardous and places of disease - thus required special deities to watch over and protect the areas. Toilet gods had dominion over fertility, health and well-being - while not often spoken of in mythologies there also exist a wide variety of toilet-demons, which toilet gods may also help keep at bay.
