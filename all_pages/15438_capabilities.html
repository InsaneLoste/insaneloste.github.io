The user possesses the capability and aptitudes to transcend one's mental restrictions, weaknesses, and cognitive impairments, allowing them to unlock, develop, and improve their mental capabilities than before. Using mind transcendence, users can develop superhuman cognitive abilities such as increased or cultivated imagination, visualization, learning, foresight, prediction, calculative skills, memory, and better right-to-left brain connectivity. Users can improve their abilities to enhanced, supernatural, or absolute levels depending on their experience, skills, and strength to surpass themselves. In some instances, users can develop psychic abilities such as telepathy, psychokinesis, extrasensory perception, astral projection, teleportation and an assortment of other psionics. Users can also apply mental transcendence to their psychic abilities to gain cosmic, universal, or nigh-omnipotent psionics.
