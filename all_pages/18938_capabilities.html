The user can create material bodies and forms for immaterial entities which generally lack physical substance in reality, granting substance and corporeality to the ubiquitous and conceptual. For example, the user can grant powerful cosmic forces physical bodies to act through in order to have communicative interactions with beings bound to less immaterial planes. This method can potentially also allow the user to extend their own presence using manifestations of their own, granting themselves a variety of different forms in the process.
