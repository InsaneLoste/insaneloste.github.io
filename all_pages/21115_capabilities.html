User can create, shape and manipulate the red string of fate, an invisible conceptual string that bonds soulmates together. They can create limitless amounts of red strings and extend them at any distance; the strings never tear apart, as they are practically indestructible. They can make the red strings become visible and touchable for others, and also choose and change anyone's soulmate; they can control the relationship of those bonded by the strings or even completely remove their bond.
