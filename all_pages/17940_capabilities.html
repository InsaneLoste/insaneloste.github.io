The user can disobey the rules of logic itself, disrupting reality and logic to achieve many astonishing and yet illogical feats. For example, the user can burn a person by throwing cold water at them. This is a very dangerous power, both for those facing the user and for the user themselves, as there is a possibility a reckless user of this ability may have their attacks backfire. This is because, unlike Logic Defiance, users of this power do not exist independently of logic itself, they merely disobey its rules. This means that they are susceptible to the consequences of the paradoxes they cause.
