Users of this ability can take on the traits of a beldam or are one, these beings are similar to hags but are exclusively malevolent in nature and often seen as demonic creatures (while hags can be both demonic and angelic, depending on the source) - hags are often associated with nature (both the good, bad and neutral parts of it) while beldams are instead an embodiment of mankind's fear of the unknown and black magic, they toil endlessly to bring ruin and often delight in their malice, giggling madly and gathering all manner of evil spirit and vermin to their aid.
