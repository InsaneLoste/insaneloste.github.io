  • May require eye contact to work, making sight impaired highly resistant and those without sight (blindness) immune.
  • In some cases, if user's killing eyes are reflected back at them (such as a mirror), it can kill the user, if not immune to the effects.
  • If the eyes are seen through a reflection of some sort, then the gaze might only be anywhere from simply knocking the person out to Petrification or Paralysis.
  • If one cannot control the power's use, visual contact must be avoided with most people, to avoid killing them involuntarily.
  • Cannot kill/harm Absolute Immortality and Flawless Indestructibility.
  • Can't kill Immortal beings, or targets that aren't technically "alive" (e.g. Artificial Life-Forms, Spirits, or the Undead) unless enraged. Other side-effects, such as Petrification, may still occur.
