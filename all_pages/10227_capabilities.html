The user can utilize the Foreign Arts, a specialized system of techniques that have abnormal properties, a different conceptual basis and origin from mainstream arts or does not exist in the known world (Extraterrestrial or Extradimensional), and thus does not follow the established "rules" or "laws" that are tied to said world. These kind of arts can come from extra-dimensional origins (other universes or planes of existence), from different planets, or even from outside the known multiverse, etc.
