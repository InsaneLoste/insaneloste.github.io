User with this ability either is or can transform into a Naga, a race of semi divine snakes in Hinduism and Buddhism who are able to shift between and combine features of human and snake in various ways. They are beings of nature (usually deities),associated with and protectors of water including rivers, lakes, seas and wells. They bring rain and thus fertility and prosperity and are guardians of treasure, but are also able to cause natural disasters such as floods and drought when angered.
