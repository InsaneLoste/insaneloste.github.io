The user possesses their own highly personalized, supernatural defense art/power. In this circumstance, whether it is a matter of inheritance or acquisition, the user is the only one in their continuity/universe who can create, shape and manipulate a particular preternatural defense capability, often being the only one in existence at a particular point. They are able to wield unique techniques, enhance their physical abilities and protect themselves, and possibly others, from opponents using supernatural defensive abilities unlike anyone else.
