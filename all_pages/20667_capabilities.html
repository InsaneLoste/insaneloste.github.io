The user is capable of performing feats of qinggong. Qinggong is a training technique in martial arts used for jumping off walls derived from the Baguazhang style but is exaggerated in performing superhuman feats in Asian Wuxia martial arts movies. This power focuses on the exaggerated Wuxia feats. With this power, the user can perform amazing feats such as leaping high into the air, fighting on poles, moving with great speed, among other possible qinggong feats. This can be done innately or through training.
