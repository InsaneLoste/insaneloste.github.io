• Can only be used when the subject is asleep.
    • Both user and target may have to be asleep at the same time.
  • Being inside the dreams of someone when they wake up may have a number of unpleasant consequences.
  • Users of Psychic Shield (highly resistant) and Psychic Immunity (impervious).
  • Cannot enter the dreams of users of Oneiric Immunity.
  • May not be able to interfere with the target's dreams.
  • User's body is vulnerable at this time.
  • As they are in another person's dream, users may be vulnerable to lucid dreamers.
