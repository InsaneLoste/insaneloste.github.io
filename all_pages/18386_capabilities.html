Perfection is a state, variously, of completeness, flawlessness, or supreme excellence. Users are in the condition, state and quality of being free from all possible forms of flaws or defects. If the user was not perfect from the beginning the traits and actions to achieving perfection must be flawless, the process of improving something until it is faultless or as close to faultless as possible, whether it be from magic, divinity, science etc. With perfection, there is nothing the wielder can't do or achieve including rejecting logic to improve themselves, despite having a flawless and limitless condition.
