The user can utilize the items and equipment of other fiction and fictional universes different from the user's own, allowing them to showcase abilities impossible in their own universe, by simply using items from other fictional universes. This may be done via portals or simply having items from other fiction at hand at all times. Due to the nature of superpowers in fiction, this allows the user of this power to have essentially every power known to exist in all of fiction as long as there is an object or tool associated with it.
