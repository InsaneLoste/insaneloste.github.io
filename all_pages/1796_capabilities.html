The user is or has the ability to take on the form of piece or art, including the visual arts - including painting, sculpting, printmaking, photography, and other visual media, literary arts/literature – including poetry, novels and short stories, among others – and the performing arts, among them being music, dance, magic, theatre and film. Users transformed form can be anatomically identical to their normal form, aside of being made of art, in which case it contains all to organs and is somewhat vulnerable to attacks. Alternately user can transform into homogeneous matter, without any part of their form being more important than the other.
