User with this ability either is or can mimic/transform into Huma bird, mythical bird of Iranian legends, that is said never to alight on the ground, and instead to live its entire life flying invisibly high above the earth, in some legends it is said to have no legs. The Huma bird is both the male and female natures in one body, each nature having one wing and one leg. It is considered to be compassionate, and a 'bird of fortune' since its shadow (or touch) is said to be auspicious.
