The user can nullify any supernatural/anomalous thing, negating it completely. This varies between individuals, considering how powerful the person using it is. At it's weakest, they may only temporarily nullify it. At seemingly average strength, they may be able to nullify it to the extent where they can't use it anymore. At its the strongest extent, they may able to remove their powers completely. Some users are even being able to absorb it. There are even those rare users capable of killing the being during the process by draining them of there powers completely until they have no power left.
