• Unless user has Infinite Supply of syringes, they can easily run out and become vulnerable in battle.
  • Syringes are very fragile and can be easily broken.
    • Fire may melt syringes if made from plastics or even glass.
  • Unless filled with a substance or drug, syringes do very little damage physically making them rather ineffective weapons on their own.
  • Unless user has Enhanced Accuracy or Projectile Enhancement, throwing syringes may not have much of an effect. 
  • Useless against Invulnerability or Immutability.
  • May require a degree of training or exposure.
  • May take time to achieve mastery.
