Like Event Manipulation, this power can alter the events of a person, place, thing or reality. However, unlike normal Event Manipulation, which only allows for manipulation of events of realities under the same timestream, this power can not only do the same thing, but can have supreme control over all universes, dimensions and realities events outside the user's timestream. No matter how outside of everything, beyonder or higher the other universes, dimensions or realities may be, the user has absolute control of any/all their events.
