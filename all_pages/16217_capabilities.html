The user can harness the power of multiple inner beasts; innate creatures that lie within users that grants them immense power, strength and ferocity, making the user an unstoppable force. This allows them to use their unique abilities to vastly increase their existing ones and/or unlock new ones. The nature of the beasts are naturally bound to the user's characteristics, experience and inner nature, which can ultimately manifest their inner demons onto potentially anything, be they wild or tame.
