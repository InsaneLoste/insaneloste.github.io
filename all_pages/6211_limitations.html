• The light created can be difficult to control, and may cause lethal backfire if not handled properly.
  • Some people may not be compatible with the power to control demonic light, which may cause the light to burn them the moment they try to control it.
  • Since the light may be inextinguishable, it can be very troublesome to control.
  • User must be careful when using the light around comrades, to prevent causing unintended casualties.
  • User may not be able to manipulate normal light, and cannot use the light for normal day purposes.
  • Demonic Light may be extinguished or repelled by holy items and substances (i.e. Holy water, crosses).
  • Low level users may not have actual manipulation of the light, and instead can only produce it and let it run wild afterwards.
  • Countered by Sacred Light Manipulation and Sacred Darkness Manipulation.
