Users embody nihilism, the belief that life is meaningless. At the most fundamental level, users would possess key physical characteristics and/or personality traits related to the concept they're personifying, in this case, a nihilistic perspective on everything around them. Some users may also possess powers related to nihilism, such as Apathy, Nothingness Manipulation/Empowerment, Negation etc. Some personifications of nihilism may also be the source of the concept in their universe.
