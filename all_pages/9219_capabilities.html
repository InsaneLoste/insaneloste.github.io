The user is able connect with the existential planes of existence to channel the primordial force/energy/domain that flows through all things. With this kind of connection to worlds beyond, users are able to cultivate an unlimited number of possibilities as much as they can interact with those worlds as if they were a part of them; because of that same connection, users are powerful conduits to the known plane of existences they are connected to, giving them access to supernatural resources of those planes.
