Users of this ability have the ability to take on the traits of a Matagot or are one, a Matagot is a spirit from French folklore that can appear as a black cat, rat, fox, dog or crow and were believed to come in both good and evil variants - though as a rule most were malevolent, a Matagot that bonded with a magician would provide varied services for them, such as granting them great wealth in the form of a single gold coin every day, providing the magician fed them properly.
