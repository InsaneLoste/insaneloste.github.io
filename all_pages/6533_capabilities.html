The user can utilize the Dimensional Arts, a specialized system of techniques based on dimension-based power. Dimensional powers can include manipulating physical dimensions, channeling otherworldly essence like energy and/or matter or even being capable of traveling through different realms. Esoteric practices related to dimensional aspects can allow one to demonstrate various types of skills. These can include energy and/or matter manipulation involving exotic substances, creating portals and/or being able to teleport or creating connections between various worlds. Although the skills involved may be dependent on a certain state of being or link to a particular dimension, the user will be able wield skills that can be unavailable to those of standardized spatial proportions or bound by their native realm of existence and its laws.
