Users can manage all kinds of cetaceans, including dolphins and whales. Holders of this ability can order cetaceans to do various things; for example, they can summon them into battle to fight their enemies or use them to infiltrate places that would normally be inaccessible to the user. Naturally, they could also communicate with them, read their thoughts and emotions and establish telepathic connections with them. More advanced users could create cetaceans out of thin air or even manipulate their genetic makeup and DNA to improve their characteristics and qualities.
