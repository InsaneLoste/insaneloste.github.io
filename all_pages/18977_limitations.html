• Despite their fearsome reputation and magical powers they are still mortal, thus could be killed by experienced hunters or warriors.
  • Like most bogeyman figures they tend to target the young and thus adults could overpower or outsmart them.
  • Tend to be non-lethal (with some exceptions), thus usually not aiming to kill a target (rather just scare or inflict minor injury).
  • Like many bogeyman figures they can only attack children who have behaved poorly (either by choice or some supernatural law).
