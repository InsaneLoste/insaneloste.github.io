Users with this power can adapt to virtually anything that the user may face by their will-based command over their existence to adapt. This power is also reactive in nature since the user is not subjugated to any limits to their adaptations, whether or not the condition of the setting requires the user to gain specific abilities that are affiliated with the need to survive or the extent and speed of their adaptations. The range of options that are available are defined only by the user's personal preferences.
