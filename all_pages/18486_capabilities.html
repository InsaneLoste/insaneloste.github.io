The user can manipulate their state of consciousness. While most instances result from biologically natural processes (e.g., sleep) or outside influence (e.g., hypnosis), individuals can bring about specialized types of awareness upon themselves. In doing so, they are capable of self-enhancements like the removal of mental blocks or attainment of supernatural abilities. Though related to State of Mind, it does not necessarily lead to personal realization. This is simply allowing one to enter into different mental modes capably.
