Users of this ability can either mimic the traits of a Hairy Dwarf or are one, a Hairy Dwarf is a type of exotic alien humanoid from modern South American legends that were seen as small ape-like creatures that would abduct humans and made use of advanced technology (such as UFOs), yet also retained a high degree of animal-like traits, being more brutish in nature than the Greys and Reptoids that would eventually come to dominate later UFO lore.
