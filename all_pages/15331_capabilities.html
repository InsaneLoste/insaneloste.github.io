Users possess superhuman proficiency and skill in wielding various/all melee weaponry. In the user's hands, any and all weapons used in direct hand-to-hand combat, whether bladed, blunt or pointed, becomes a tool of limitless potential. A master practitioner of this ability can even turn various ranged weapons and small objects into unconventional melee weapons. The user is also capable of mastering the martial arts that focus on melee implements and may turn any available object into an impromptu weapon. The user's proficiency has reached such a degree of finesse, that they are even capable of targeting, and attacking pressure points or other weaknesses with incredible ease.
