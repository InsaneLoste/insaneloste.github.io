The user can create any type of artificial, organic and inorganic matter, as well as any object, either from nothing, by using existing sources of matter and energy or through other immaterial sources. They can shape created matter into any form they wish and specify what type of material or layers of substances (for example, make layers of matter to form food, technology, clothing, even buildings etc...), or quantity and subatomic structure with some advanced users being able to manipulate the matter they created such items.
