The user is made up of absolutely everything of any kind giving them a body of omniversal scale and beyond. The users' form is anatomically made of the totality of all things. The user possesses power and authority over everything that happens within themselves allowing them take on any form desired. The user can also micromanage each and every life-form and type of life, etc. The lifeforms that inhabit them are often an extension of their being, often acting as their immune system.
