• Each variant has a specific means by which to destroy it, a "skin-zombie" requires one to break the skin, a "blood-zombie" must be made to bleed, a "flesh-zombie" must be deeply cut or have flesh removed, a "bone-zombie" must have a fractured bone, and a "mole-zombie" is only vulnerable at a mole on the body.
  • Unable to bend due to extremely stiff limbs, thus cannot enter low doors (thus many doors used to be made low in order to stop the creatures from entering homes).
