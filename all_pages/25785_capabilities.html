The users of this ability either is or can become a spirit/spiritual being of transcendent/godlike power. Users of this possess incredible power above the class of all manner of spiritual beings, having unimaginable powers and status beyond that of any spirit no matter their nature. Users have absolute supernatural and spiritual powers and are able to effectively control all of existence as much as they have total lordship over all things regardless of if they are mundane, supernatural, living, dead, and so on.
