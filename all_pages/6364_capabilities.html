The power to use magic born from the concept of destruction itself; Destruction Magic is capable of being moulded into almost any Destructive attack so long as the user's strength matches what they want to cause, in some cases not even having that stop them. The level of power can range from simple explosive attacks, destroying inanimate objects, disintegration of bodies, or even destruction of concepts on a high level. At true perfection, users could theoretically destroy anything and everything, causing a Totality Collapse.
