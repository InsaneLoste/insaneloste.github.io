The user can create, shape and manipulate geochemistry, the chemical aspects of a planet’s minerals and rocks. This includes the elemental/periodic traits of materials in/on planets. A planet’s chemical composition comes from a combination of partially melted materials from its interior mixing together. This results in various types of elements, compounds and natural phenomena related to said substances. Controlling a power like this allows one to manifest various capabilities connected to a particular planet. They can trigger events like geological formations and energy emissions due to chemical reactions. They can cause various types of chemical substances to be brought into existence. They can even utilize naturally-occurring chemicals to whatever end they need.
