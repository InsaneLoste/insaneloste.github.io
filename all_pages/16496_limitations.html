• May require knowledge of the creature into which one transforms.
  • User gains the limitations and weaknesses of the assumed form.
  • Magic Immunity - opponent/victims could have immunity to the users magic.
  • Magic Negation - opponent/victim could cancel/undo the users magic
  • Magic Destruction - opponent/victim could destroy the users magic.
  • Shapeshifting Negation - opponent/victim could undo the users shapeshifting and force their true appearance to be exposed.
  • Absorption Magic - opponent/victim could absorb the users magic entirely leaving them powerless.
  • Mythical Beast Manipulation - user will be forced to do the bidding of the victim/opponent.
