Users possess superhuman proficiency and skill in wielding various/all ranged weaponry, demonstrating peerless aptitude that rivals any individual proficiency. In the user's hands, any and all weapons used in mid-to-long-ranged engagements, whether it be primitive, advanced, or alien hardware, becomes a tool of limitless potential. A master practitioner of this ability can even turn various melee implements or small objects into impromptu ranged weaponry. The user is also capable of mastering intensive techniques that may bend or break the limits of conventional projectile weapons. The user's proficiency has reached such a degree of precision, that they are even capable of targeting, and attacking pressure points or other weaknesses with incredible ease.
