Users of this ability are able to control canines, including as dogs, wolves, coyotes, foxes, African wild dogs, dholes, dingoes, etc. They may sick canines onto opponents, or they can have canines help the user in some way. The user can also use canines to spy on others. The controlled dogs may be able to bond with the user, and the user can also empathize as well as communicate with dogs as well.
