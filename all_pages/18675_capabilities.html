The user possesses their own highly personalized, supernatural support art/power. In this circumstance, whether it is a matter of inheritance or acquisition, the user is the only one in their continuity/universe who can create, shape and manipulate a particular preternatural support capability, often being the only one in existence at a particular point. They are able to wield unique techniques, enhance their physical abilities and support themselves, and possibly others, using supernatural assistance abilities unlike anyone else.
