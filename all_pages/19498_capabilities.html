The user can utilize the Possession Arts, a specialized system of techniques based around possessive power. Possession can come about through various methods: spiritual detachment, mental domination, physical merging, remote entity manipulation. Through any one or a combination of these various methods, users can physically, mentally and/or spiritually overtake targets, whether they are living or non-living. Along with specialized skills that allow one to possess their targets, there are also unique practices that enable a person supernatural ability related to the act. One can learn to absorb the knowledge and/or abilities of the target they control. They could enhance the inherent powers of the host, allowing for newfound abilities previously unavailable to either being. Users can learn to possess more than one host at once or any type of host. One can even take on a continuously evolving state-of-being thanks to every possession that occurs.
