Users of this ability can either mimic the traits of a Domsten Blob or are one, the Domsten Blobs are modern Swedish UFO legends related to a 1958 alien encounter story in which two men claimed to be attacked by a group of strange gray blob-like creatures that tried to pull them into a small spacecraft - according to the men the creatures lacked notable features and were no larger than four feet tall but possessed a near-unbreakable grip, frightening speed and could dodge their attempts to fight back, almost as if they could read the men's thoughts.
