• Lower vampires are unable to possess this ability.
  • Users are unable to affect other types of beings with this ability outside vampires themselves.
  • Users of this great power may have absolute control over others of their own kind, however, they can be temporally overthrown by several major vampires at once if caught off guard. This is only temporary as these vampires or beings of this power are naturally more powerful.
  • Some users of this power can not be killed because it could take any created vampires by them with them to the grave, effectively killing them as well. This may not always be the case.
