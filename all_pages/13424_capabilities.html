Users of this ability are capable of harnessing the incredible magical potential of the jungle, they can summon and control any animal or plant that may be found in the jungle (which in fiction often includes lions, dinosaurs and other creatures not found in real-world jungles), they may call on native tribes for aid or utilize these tribe's magic in addition to their own, they often have telepathic influence over the entire jungle and can tell if disturbances are occurring and act swiftly to stop them. They may alter weather, mimicking the weather often found in jungles and they may also take on the qualities of animals, spirits or concepts found in the jungle.
