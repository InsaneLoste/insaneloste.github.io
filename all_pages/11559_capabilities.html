The user can utilize the Healing Arts, a specialized system of techniques based around restorative power. Proper treatment of patients often requires specialized knowledge of anatomy, chemistry and biological function. Through training and practice, one can master the necessary treatment techniques for healing almost any ailment or injury. This, however, can go far beyond mere recovery methods. Supernatural studies in health can yield potent benefits including accelerated convalescence, enhanced vigor in patients and even the ability to grant newfound abilities in others. In the most extreme cases, one could potentially resurrect the dead. Application of beneficial effects can be extended to the user as well, allowing them to heighten their own bodies.
