The user can gain powers by symbiotically hosting or linking to a bloodline entity. Within certain genealogical groups, there are individuals who can bond with specific beings. These symbiotes possess a unique connection to a particular bloodline; as such, people with that unique ancestry can bond with them, acquiring abilities unavailable to others. Potentially, one can acquire symbiotic powers by being clones or having undergone gene-splicing. Just as with most forms of Symbiosis, it can come with a price.
