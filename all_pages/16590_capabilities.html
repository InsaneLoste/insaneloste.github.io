Users of this ability can either mimic the traits of a Narigamao or are one, Narigama are yokai formed when iron kettles or cauldrons gain life due to very old age, Narigama can shift form into strange pot-like monsters with clawed limbs and an ability to emit fire, however they are most famous for their ability to predict the future - via listening to the sounds the Narigama makes people can predict anything from the weather to good or bad fortunes.
