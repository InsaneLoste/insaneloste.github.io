• Metal Manipulation can over-ride the user's control.
  • Controlled lead is affected by everything that normal lead would be, although the user is perfectly able to use their power to return the metal in its original shape and/or controlling it even under these forces:
    • melt under extreme heat (Fire Manipulation or Magma Manipulation).
    • turn brittle and shatter under extreme cold (Freezing).
  • Lead is a particularly soft metal and doesn't hold its shape well.
  • Lead is highly poisonous and can harm the user unless they have resistance to its toxicity.
  • Distance, mass, precision, etc. depend upon of the knowledge, skill, and strength of the user, and their power's natural limits.
