The user has authority and duty over the abyss, the realm where souls go into an eternal void to endure absolute isolation from everything in existence. Some would call this a fate worse then even Hell itself, as souls are forever doomed to wallow alone without love or peace, forever losing bits and pieces of who they are into eternal oblivion until they become nothing. Some users gain control over a certain part of abyss where they command everything and everyone in the area and gain a lot of power whenever they are in the void itself.
