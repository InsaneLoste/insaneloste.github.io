The user is able to perform a legendary form of magic that allows the user to cast magical spells and feats through earth and rock. Depending on the verse Earth magic comes from two major domains: the stone, minerals, crystals, and gems of the land, and the flora and fauna of the living world. Earth magic draws on the power and energy within the land itself, embodying the enduring strength of stone and the vibrant energy of new life. This source of magic is normally strongest deep underground or in the heart of a forest. Mythwise the Earth is associated with strength, endurance, healing, stubbornness, growth, and balance
