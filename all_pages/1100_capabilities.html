A user with this ability either is or can transform into an Amemasu. An amemasu is a giant whale- or fish-like creature/monster in which are known for capsizing boats, creating earthquakes, and causing other natural disasters. Some of the largest amemasus are said to live in Lake Mashū and Lake Shikotsu in Hokkaidō, with smaller ones inhabiting lakes throughout northern areas of Honshu. They can also be able to take on a human appearance, usually of a beautiful woman, in order to lure young men to their deaths.
